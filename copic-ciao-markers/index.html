Copic Ciao Markers: High Quality for Beginners and Beyond

By Colin · 02 Feb 2025 · 3 min read

Enjoy Copic quality for less with Copic Ciao Markers - blendable, refillable, and perfect for illustration, manga, and craft projects. Order yours today.

Copic Ciao Markers are the entry-level series from the Japanese brand that has been making iconic Copic Markers since 1987. While they’re designed with beginners and younger artists in mind, they carry the same high-quality ink, durability, and refillable design that make Copics so popular with professionals. Safe, vibrant, and versatile, the Ciao line is often the first step into the world of marker art — and for good reason.

Copic Ciao Markers: Safe, Durable, and Artist-Friendly

Copic Ciao markers use alcohol-based ink that’s non-toxic and conforms to ASTM D-4236 safety standards. They’re completely safe for young artists while still delivering professional-grade performance. The ink is vibrant, consistent, and blendable, and because each marker can be refilled and fitted with replaceable nibs, a single marker can last for years.

Double-Ended for Versatility

Like all Copics, the Ciao features two nibs:

  • Medium broad nib – great for filling larger areas like backgrounds.
  • Super brush nib – responds to pressure, allowing thin or thick strokes in one movement.

The Ciao’s narrower barrel makes it easier for small hands to grip, while the child-safe caps have tiny air holes to prevent choking in case they’re swallowed.

Color Range and Sets of Copic Ciao Markers

Copic Ciao markers are available in 180 colors, including a colorless blender for smooth gradients and effects. They come in a variety of sets:

  • Small starter sets (4–6 pieces)
  • Mid-size sets (9–36 pieces)
  • Large collections (up to 72 pieces)

Each set is curated so colors work well together, whether you’re sketching, doodling, or creating full illustrations.

Want vibrant, blendable color without breaking the bank? Copic Ciao Markers give you pro-quality performance in a beginner-friendly design—get yours today!

Artist Feedback

Most artists praise the Ciao for its durability, smooth blending, and ease of use. The few complaints usually come from specific use cases — for example, very fine lines may lack opacity, or glossy paper can cause ink to bleed. While they are the cheapest Copics, they still cost more than typical markers. That said, their refillable design makes them far more cost-effective over time.

FAQs About Copic Ciao Markers

Q: Are the markers refillable?

Yes. You can refill them using Copic Ink refills, and each refill can top up a marker multiple times.

Q: How many colors are in the Ciao range?

There are 180 colors, including a colorless blender.

Q: Are Copic Ciao Markers good for beginners?

Absolutely. The smaller barrel and child-safe caps make them comfortable and safe for younger or less experienced artists.

Q: How do they compare to Copic Sketch markers?

The Ciao is cheaper but offers fewer colors (180 vs. 358). The barrel is round instead of oval, and slightly smaller.

Q: Can they be used on glossy paper?

They can, but the ink may bleed. They perform best on smooth, bleed-proof marker paper.

Final Thoughts

Copic Ciao Markers may be designed for younger artists, but their quality makes them just as appealing to adults. They’re a safe, long-lasting, and premium option for beginners, and a cost-friendly alternative for professionals who don’t need the full color range of the Sketch series.

If you’re okay with the round, slightly smaller barrel and can find your desired colors in the Ciao line, they’re a smart buy - whether you’re just starting out or adding to a professional toolkit.
