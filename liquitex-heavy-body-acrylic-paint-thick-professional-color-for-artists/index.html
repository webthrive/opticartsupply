Liquitex Professional heavy body acrylic paint is unique because its formula combines 100% acrylic resin with intense pigments for brilliant, lightfast color. It dries to a flexible, satin finish that resists cracking, fading, and yellowing.

Exceptional Texture Retention

Every mark you make stays visible. The Liquitex acrylic heavy body paint thick body captures movement and brush direction, giving your work a dimensional, expressive quality that remains stable once dry.

Consistent Color Accuracy

Because of its minimal wet-to-dry color shift, your mixed tones remain true from start to finish. This feature allows precise layering and glazing without unexpected dullness or tone loss.

Durable Film and Smooth Handling

After drying, the paint forms a smooth, flexible film that resists damage and environmental changes. It glides easily on brushes and palette knives while maintaining excellent coverage and structure.

How Does It Perform Across Different Techniques and Surfaces?

Liquitex heavy body acrylic paint performs consistently on canvas, wood, paper, and panel surfaces. It adapts to your technique, whether you use thick impasto or delicate glazes.

  • Controlled Blending: The natural surface drag helps blend edges without losing precision, allowing smooth gradients and subtle tone shifts.
  • Perfect for Knives and Brushes: Brushes spread the paint evenly, while knives sculpt layers that dry firm and expressive.
  • Compatible With Liquitex Mediums: Mix it with gels, modelling pastes, or retarders to control drying time, sheen, or texture.
  • Reliable Coverage: Each layer holds rich color without dulling or bleeding, creating solid base coats and vibrant finishes.
  • Long-Lasting Stability: Once cured, the paint maintains elasticity, preventing cracks or brittleness over time.
