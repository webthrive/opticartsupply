Speedball Artista Potter's Wheel: A Perfect Choice for Potters of All Levels

By Colin · 21 May 2025 · 3 min read

Buy the Speedball Artista Pottery Wheel and improve your ceramics practice with a portable, high-performance wheel. Ideal for artists of all skill levels.

If you're into pottery, you know that a good potter’s wheel can make all the difference. Whether you’re a beginner looking for your first wheel or an experienced artist in need of a portable option, the Speedball Artista Potter's Wheel is a solid choice. It’s lightweight, easy to transport, and packs a punch with its performance. This article breaks down everything you need to know before buying the Artista, so you can decide if it's the right fit for your creative needs.

Why Choose the Speedball Artista Potter's Wheel?

Pottery wheels come in many shapes, sizes, and prices. The Artista stands out because it is compact, affordable, and great for both beginners and experienced potters. Let’s take a closer look at what makes it special.

Compact & Portable

One of the best things about the Artista is its size. It’s designed as a tabletop wheel, which means you don’t need a dedicated pottery studio to use it. If you live in a small space or want a wheel that’s easy to move, this one is perfect. At just 35 lbs, you can carry it to workshops, classes, or even outdoors to work in natural light.

It also features removable splash pans, making cleanup easy. These pans help contain clay splatters and water, so you don’t have to worry about making a mess.

Strong Motor & Smooth Operation

Don’t let its small size fool you—this wheel is powerful. It has a 1/3 HP motor that runs smoothly and quietly. The variable speed control allows you to adjust the speed from 0 to 220 RPM, giving you full control over your work.

The 11-inch diameter wheel head is large enough for most projects, and with a 25 lb centering capacity, you can comfortably throw medium-sized pieces. Whether you're making bowls, plates, or vases, this wheel can handle it.

Easy to Use

For beginners, a good pottery wheel should be easy to control. The Speedball Artista Potter's Wheel comes with hand-controlled speed adjustment, meaning you don’t need to use a foot pedal—though one is available if you prefer. This makes it ideal for people who want a simple, straightforward experience without extra accessories.

If you're already used to a foot pedal, don’t worry! Speedball offers a separate foot pedal attachment, which can be plugged in to give you a traditional pottery wheel experience.

Customizable & Expandable

The Artista is designed to grow with you. As your skills improve, you might want to customize your setup. Speedball offers optional accessories like:

  • Folding Legs – Convert the Artista from a tabletop wheel to a free-standing one
  • Static Legs – Make the wheel taller for a more comfortable working height
  • Foot Pedal – Switch from hand-controlled speed to a foot-operated experience

These add-ons make the Artista a versatile choice that can adapt to your changing needs.

Who Is This Speedball Artista Potter's Wheel Best For?

The Speedball Artista is great for many types of users, but it’s especially well-suited for:

  • Beginners – It’s simple to use and doesn’t require much setup
  • Artists with Limited Space – Its compact design is perfect for small studios or home use
  • Teachers & Workshop Instructors – Lightweight and portable, it’s easy to bring to classes
  • Experienced Potters Needing a Secondary Wheel – If you already have a full-sized wheel but want something smaller and portable, the Artista is a great backup option

However, if you're looking to throw large or heavy pieces over 25 lbs, you may need a wheel with a stronger motor.

Where to Buy the Speedball Artista Potter’s Wheel

  • Blick Art Materials
  • Amazon

Before purchasing, compare prices and check for discounts or bundle deals. Some retailers offer the wheel with accessories included, which can save you money.

Final Thoughts: Is the Speedball Artista Potter’s Wheel Worth It?

If you're looking for a reliable, affordable, and portable pottery wheel, the Speedball Artista is a fantastic choice. It’s easy to use, well-built, and offers great value for both beginners and experienced potters.

Its compact design makes it perfect for small spaces, while its powerful motor ensures smooth and controlled throwing. With the option to add accessories, it can be customized to fit your needs as you grow as an artist.

Whether you're just starting your pottery journey or need a versatile second wheel, the Speedball Artista Potter’s Wheel is a smart investment that can help bring your creative ideas to life.
