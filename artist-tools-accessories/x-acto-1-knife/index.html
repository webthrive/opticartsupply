How to Use the X-Acto Knife Safely and Effectively

Step 1: Choose the Right Blade

Select the appropriate blade for your project. Use the #11 blade for detailed cutting or swap it for a #10 or #16 blade for specialized cuts.

Step 2: Install the Blade

  • Unscrew the collar on the knife handle.
  • Carefully insert the blade into the slot.
  • Tighten the collar to secure the blade.
  • Always handle the blade with caution.

Step 3: Cut with Control

  • Use a cutting mat to protect your surface.
  • Apply light, even pressure - let the X-Acto #1 Knife do the work.
  • For curved cuts, move the material instead of the knife for smoother lines.

Step 4: Store Safely

  • Always replace the safety cap after use.
  • Store extra blades in a safe container.

Tips for Best Results

  • Keep Blades Sharp: Replace dull blades regularly for cleaner cuts.
  • Use a Cutting Mat: Protects your surfaces and prolongs blade life.
  • Cut Away from Your Body: Always cut in a direction away from your hands.
  • Apply Gentle Pressure: Heavy force isn’t necessary—let the sharp blade do the work.
