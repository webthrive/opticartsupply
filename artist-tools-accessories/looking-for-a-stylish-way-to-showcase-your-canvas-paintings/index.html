Blick Wood Floater Frames – Modern, Elegant Art Presentation

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Showcase your canvas with Blick Wood Floater Frames - high-quality, stylish, and built to give artwork a professional floating effect.

Looking for a stylish way to showcase your canvas paintings? Blick Wood Floater Frames are the perfect solution. These beautifully crafted frames give your artwork a modern, professional look by creating the illusion that your canvas is "floating" within the frame. Whether you're displaying gallery-wrapped canvases or handmade art, these frames will make your work stand out.

Why Choose Blick Wood Floater Frames?

1. Modern, Minimalist Design

Unlike traditional frames that cover the edges of your canvas, Blick Floater Frames create a clean, modern look by allowing your artwork to appear as if it’s floating. This design adds depth and dimension, making your art the star of the show.

2. Perfect for Gallery-Wrapped Canvases

These frames are designed specifically for gallery-wrapped canvases where the edges are part of the artwork and no staples are visible. This means you can display the full canvas without hiding any details.

3. Handcrafted Quality

Each Blick Wood Floater Frames is handmade with careful attention to detail. The wood is finely finished, and the gold leaf frames are created using an "Old World" technique that leaves beautiful, natural lines between each gold leaf.

4. Easy to Assemble

Attaching your canvas to the frame is simple. The frame easily mounts to the stretcher bars of your canvas from the back using screws (not included). No complicated assembly or professional tools needed.

5. Versatile Sizes and Finishes

Available in various popular sizes and colors, Blick Floater Frames can match any style or artwork. Whether you prefer classic wood tones or elegant gold leaf, there’s a frame to complement your piece.

Key Features

  • Floating Effect: A 3/8" gap between the canvas and the frame creates a striking floating appearance.
  • Rabbet Depth: The 7/8" deep rabbet is perfect for canvases that are 3/4" to 7/8" thick.
  • Hand-Finished Wood: Smooth, polished finishes in various colors.
  • Open Back Design: Frames come without backing, glazing, or hardware—ideal for stretched canvases and panels.

How to Use Blick Wood Floater Frames?

Step 1: Measure Your Artwork

  • Measure the depth of your canvas, including any mounting materials.
  • Choose a frame with a rabbet depth that matches or exceeds this measurement.

Step 2: Position the Canvas in the Frame

  • Place your canvas into the floater frame, ensuring it’s centered.
  • The 3/8" space between the canvas and the frame will create the floating effect.

Step 3: Secure the Canvas

  • Use screws (not included) to attach the stretcher bars of the canvas to the frame from the back.
  • Tighten screws carefully to avoid damaging the frame or canvas.

Step 4: Hang and Display

Add hanging hardware if needed and display your artwork proudly.

Creative Uses for Wood Floater Frames

1. Art Galleries and Exhibitions

Floater frames give artwork a sleek, polished look perfect for gallery displays. The clean design draws all attention to the artwork.

2. Home Décor

Display your favorite canvas paintings in your home with a minimalist, modern frame. These frames fit beautifully into any space, from living rooms to home offices.

3. Professional Artist Presentations

Want to sell your art or showcase it professionally? Floater frames make your work look high-end and ready for exhibitions or sales.

4. Custom Projects

Use these frames for handmade canvases, mixed media art, or even textile art for a contemporary presentation.

Available Styles and Sizes

  • Standard Wood Finishes: Classic black, white, and natural wood finishes to suit any style.
  • Gold Leaf Frames: Hand-finished using traditional methods for a timeless, elegant look.
  • Variety of Sizes: Fits standard canvas sizes with rabbet depths of 7/8", perfect for most gallery-wrapped canvases.
