  • DIY Repairs – Tackle small fixes that require precision cuts.
  • School Projects – Ideal for posters, displays, and creative designs.

3. Multiple Styles for Every Need

X-Acto offers several blade types to match the demands of different projects:

  • Standard #11 Blades: For general-purpose cutting.

These blades are crafted to last. Whether you choose the Stainless Steel or X-Life version, you can count on blades that stay sharp and resist wear.

How to Use X-Acto #11 Blades Safely?

Step 1: Choose the Right Blade

Select the blade that best fits your project’s needs. For everyday cuts, go with the Standard or Stainless Steel blade. For more demanding tasks, the X-Life or Z-Series is ideal.

Step 2: Install the Blade Carefully

  • Unscrew the knife handle, insert the blade, and tighten securely.
  • Always handle the blade by the dull side to avoid injury.

Step 3: Cut with Control

  • Apply gentle pressure; let the blade do the work.
  • Use a cutting mat to protect your surfaces and extend blade life.

Step 4: Store Safely

  • Keep unused blades in a secure case or holder.
  • Dispose of dull blades properly to prevent accidents.
