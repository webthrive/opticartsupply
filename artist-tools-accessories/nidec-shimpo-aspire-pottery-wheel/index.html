Nidec Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel – Compact & Professional

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 2 min read

Create anywhere with the Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel—lightweight, durable, and designed for smooth, reliable ceramic shaping.

If pottery sparks your creativity, having the right tools can make all the difference. The Nidec Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel is a fantastic option for both beginners and experienced potters. Compact, lightweight, and versatile, this tabletop wheel fits perfectly into any studio or classroom. Let’s explore what makes the Nidec Shimpo Aspire a popular choice among pottery enthusiasts.

What Is the Nidec Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel?

The Nidec Shimpo Aspire is a portable pottery wheel designed for convenience and functionality. Despite its compact size, it boasts impressive features that make it suitable for various pottery projects.

Available Models:

  • Hand Lever Model: Speed range 0–230 rpm
  • Foot Pedal Model: Speed range 0–250 rpm for precise control

Both models include:

  • 1/3 HP, 100W DC motor
  • 7" alloy wheel head
  • Removable one-piece splash pan
  • Two 9¾" bats

You can start throwing right away!

Why Choose the Nidec Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel?

Compact and Lightweight

The Aspire’s small size and low weight make it easy to move around. Whether you’re setting up in a studio or transporting it to a classroom, it’s hassle-free.

Powerful Performance

Despite its compact design, the Aspire can handle up to 20 lbs of clay. Its 1/3 HP motor ensures smooth, consistent operation.

Customizable Speed Options

Choose between the hand lever or foot pedal model based on your speed control preference.

Easy to Clean

The removable splash pan allows for quick, easy cleaning after each session.

Durable and Reliable

Backed by a five-year manufacturer’s warranty, the Aspire is built to last.

Ready to take your pottery skills to the next level? Explore the Nidec Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel today!

How to Choose the Right Aspire Model

Hand Lever Model

  • Simpler setup
  • Speed range: 0–230 rpm
  • Ideal for beginners or minimalists

Foot Pedal Model

  • Hands-free speed adjustment
  • Speed range: 0–250 rpm
  • Great for advanced control

Tip: You can upgrade later using the Foot Pedal Conversion Kit if needed!

How to Use the Nidec Shimpo Aspire Pottery Wheel

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Setup: Place the wheel on a stable surface and attach splash pan and bats.
  2. Centering: Center up to 20 lbs of clay.
  3. Adjust Speed: Use the hand lever or foot pedal to control rotation.
  4. Create: Shape your clay into beautiful pieces.
  5. Clean Up: Remove the splash pan and bats for easy cleaning.
