What Makes BodyMark Temporary Tattoo Markers?

1. Bright, Long-Lasting Colors

These markers come in bold, vivid shades that pop on your skin. The ink is designed to stay bright and beautiful for up to three days, making them perfect for weekend events or parties.

2. Flexible Tips for Creative Control

Whether you're drawing delicate lines or bold designs, the flexible tip lets you create thin strokes or wide swaths with ease. Perfect for detailed art or big, bold tattoos.

3. Skin-Safe and Cosmetic-Grade Ink

BIC designed these markers with cosmetic-quality ink that’s low odor and safe for skin. Just remember to apply on clean, dry skin and avoid sensitive areas like your eyes or lips.

4. Easy to Remove

When you’re ready for a fresh design, simply wash the tattoo off with soap and water. Some colors might take a few washes, but it’s all part of the fun!

How to Use BodyMark Temporary Tattoo Markers?

Step 1: Prep Your Canvas (Your Skin!)

  • Start with clean, dry skin for the best results.
  • Avoid applying on broken, irritated, or sensitive skin.

Step 2: Get Creative

  • Use the flexible tip to draw thin lines for detailed art or thick strokes for bold designs.
  • Mix colors for a vibrant, multi-color look!

Step 3: Let It Dry

  • Give your design a moment to dry—no smudges here!

Step 4: Show It Off!

  • Enjoy your body art for up to three days.

Step 5: Ready for a Change?

  • Wash off with soap and water. Some designs may need a few washes to fully disappear.

Tips for Best Results

  • Test First: If you have sensitive skin, test the BodyMark Temporary Tattoo Markers on a small patch before using.
  • Avoid Fabric: The ink can stain clothes, so be careful when wearing light fabrics.
  • Layer Colors: Layer colors for cool effects or shading!
  • Add Accessories: Combine your designs with body glitter or gems for extra flair.

Important Safety Info

  • For external use only.
  • Do not use near eyes or lips.
  • Not recommended for broken or irritated skin.
  • Stop using if you experience redness or irritation.
  • Keep away from fabric—the ink can stain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are BIC BodyMark Markers safe for all skin types?

A: They are cosmetic-grade and safe for most skin, but they aren’t tested on sensitive skin. Always test on a small patch first if you’re unsure.

Q: How long do the designs last?

A: Designs can last up to three days, depending on how often the area is washed or rubbed.

Q: How do I remove the ink?

A: Use soap and water. Some colors may need a few washes to fully remove.

Q: Can I use BodyMark Temporary Tattoo Markers on fabric or other surfaces?

A: These markers are designed for skin. The ink can stain fabric or other materials, so avoid using them on anything else.
