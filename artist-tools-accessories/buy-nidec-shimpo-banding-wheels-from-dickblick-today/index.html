What Are Banding Wheels?

Banding wheels are rotating platforms used by potters and ceramic artists for decorating, sculpting, or painting pottery. They allow for smooth 360-degree rotation, making it easier to add intricate details or even out surfaces.

Applications of Banding Wheels

How to Choose the Right Banding Wheel?

When selecting a Nidec Shimpo banding wheel, consider these factors:

Size and Weight

Larger wheels are more stable for big projects, while smaller ones are easier to transport.

Material

Cast iron models are heavier and more stable, while aluminum options are lighter but still sturdy.

Budget

Nidec Shimpo offers models across various price points, ensuring there’s something for everyone.

How to take proper Care of the Nidec Shimpo Banding Wheel?

Regular Cleaning

Wipe the wheel after each use to remove debris.

Proper Storage

Avoid Overloading
