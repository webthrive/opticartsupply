Blick Mesh Zipper Bags: The Perfect Storage Solution for Artists, Students, and Travelers

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Staying organized can be a challenge, especially when you have a lot of small items like pens, brushes, papers, or travel essentials. That’s where Blick Mesh Zipper Bags come in. These handy see-through pouches help you keep your supplies neat, protected, and easy to find. Whether you're an artist, student, or frequent traveler, these bags are a game changer for keeping everything in one place.

In this guide, we’ll take a closer look at why these bags are so useful, how you can use them, and where to get them.

Why Blick Mesh Zipper Bags Are a Must-Have

There are a lot of storage options out there, but Blick Mesh Zipper Bags stand out for several reasons.

1. Transparent for Easy Organization

One of the biggest perks of these bags is their see-through mesh design. Unlike solid-colored pouches where you have to open them up and dig around to find what you need, these bags let you see the contents instantly. This is especially helpful for artists and students who carry a lot of small supplies.

2. Built to Last

Made from durable PVC material, these bags are designed to handle regular use. Whether you're tossing them in a backpack, stacking them in your art studio, or carrying them while traveling, they hold up well. They’re much sturdier than plastic or flimsy ziplock bags that tear easily.

3. Secure Zipper Closure

Nothing is worse than reaching into your bag and realizing your pens, brushes, or papers have scattered everywhere. The sturdy zipper closure ensures everything stays securely inside, so you won’t have to deal with a mess. Plus, the zipper glides smoothly, so it won’t get stuck or break after a few uses.

4. Comes in Different Sizes

No matter what you need to store, there’s a Blick Mesh Zipper Bag that fits the job. These bags come in a variety of sizes, from small pouches for tiny tools like erasers and clips to larger ones that can hold sketchbooks, documents, or even a tablet.

5. Lightweight and Portable

Despite being durable, these bags are lightweight and easy to carry. They won’t add unnecessary weight to your bag, making them perfect for students heading to school, artists on the go, or travelers who need to keep essentials neatly packed.

Creative Ways to Use Blick Mesh Zipper Bags

You might think of these bags as simple storage pouches, but they can be used for so much more. Here are some great ways to make the most of them:

For Artists and Crafters

  • Brush and Pencil Storage – Keep different types of brushes, pencils, and markers in separate bags for easy access.
  • Paint and Ink Organization – Store small tubes of watercolor, acrylic paints, or ink bottles without worrying about spills.
  • Sketchbook and Paper Protection – Use larger bags to keep your paper and sketchbooks clean and free from damage.

For Students and Office Workers

  • Stationery Organizer – Store pens, highlighters, rulers, erasers, and sticky notes in one place.
  • Paper and Document Holder – Keep important notes, flashcards, or reports safely tucked away.
  • Electronics Storage – Use a bag to hold phone chargers, USB drives, and earphones to keep them from getting tangled.

For Travelers

  • Toiletry Bag – Store travel-sized shampoos, lotions, toothbrushes, and other essentials without worrying about leaks.
  • Tech Accessories – Keep your phone, tablet, or camera accessories in one secure pouch.
  • Important Documents – Protect your passport, plane tickets, and travel itinerary from getting lost or damaged.
