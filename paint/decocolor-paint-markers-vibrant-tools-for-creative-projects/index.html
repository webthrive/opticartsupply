What Are Decocolor Paint Markers?

The Paint Markers are premium-quality markers designed to provide opaque, permanent coverage on a wide variety of surfaces. Manufactured by Marvy Uchida, these markers are known for their rich pigments, smooth application, and long-lasting results.

Tip Varieties

  • Extra Fine Tips: Perfect for detailed work like lettering and intricate designs.
  • Fine and Broad Tips: Ideal for covering larger areas or bold strokes.
  • Chisel Tips: Great for creating varied line widths.

Vibrant Colors

Non-Toxic Formula

Applications of Decocolor Markers

Whether you're a professional artist or a DIY enthusiast, Decocolor Paint Markers can help bring your creative ideas to life.

  • Customizing Shoes and Clothing: Add unique designs to sneakers, jackets, or hats.

Tips for Using Decocolor Markers

To get the most out of your markers, keep these tips in mind:

  • Shake Well: Always shake your marker before use to ensure even ink distribution.
  • Prime the Tip: Press the tip down on a scrap surface until the ink flows.
  • Seal Your Artwork: Apply a clear sealant on non-porous surfaces for added protection.
  • Store Horizontally: Helps prevent the markers from drying out.

Frequently Asked Questions on Decocolor Paint Markers

1. Can the markers be used on fabric?

Yes, they work well on fabric, but it's recommended to seal the design with heat or a fabric sealant for better durability.

2. Are these markers refillable?

No, Decocolor M<arkers are not refillable. However, their ink lasts a long time with proper storage.

3. How do I clean the tips if they get clogged?

Wipe the tip with a damp cloth and gently press it down to reactivate the ink flow.

4. Are Decocolor Paint markers safe for kids?

While they are non-toxic, always supervise children and check the product labels for age recommendations.
