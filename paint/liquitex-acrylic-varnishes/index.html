Liquitex Acrylic Varnishes: Professional Protection for Acrylic Art

By Colin · 27 May 2025 · 2 min read

Worried about your acrylic paintings fading over time? Shop Liquitex Varnishes, which add a clear, protective finish and lock in color, resisting UV damage.

Liquitex Acrylic Varnishes are the perfect finishing touch for acrylic artworks, providing long-lasting protection against harsh elements and UV damage. Made from 100% acrylic polymer emulsions, these varnishes form a flexible yet durable film that safeguards your creations while enhancing their appearance.

Available in a range of finishes and over 25 size options, Liquitex Varnishes dry clear and resist yellowing or fogging, ensuring your artwork remains vibrant for years to come. Shop now at Blick Art Materials for superior protection and professional results.

Why Choose Liquitex Acrylic Varnishes?

1. Professional-Grade Protection

These varnishes create a permanent, non-removable layer that shields your artwork from dirt, dust, moisture, and harmful UV rays.

2. Flexible Yet Hard Finish

Their unique formula ensures a durable coating that adapts to temperature and humidity changes without cracking or peeling.

3. Crystal Clear and Non-Yellowing

These varnishes dry completely clear, maintaining your artwork’s original colors and details without yellowing or fogging over time.

4. Versatile Finishes

Choose from a variety of finishes, including:

  • Gloss: Adds a shiny, reflective surface for a vibrant look.
  • Matte: Provides a subdued, non-reflective finish for subtle elegance.
  • Satin: Combines the best of gloss and matte for a balanced effect.

Available Options: Liquitex Acrylic Varnishes come in over 25 sizes, making them ideal for projects of any scale, from small canvases to large installations.

Ideal Uses

  • Fine Art Paintings: Protect and enhance your acrylic masterpieces.
  • Mixed Media Projects: Ensure durability for works combining various materials.
  • Murals and Installations: Guard large-scale artworks against environmental damage.

How to Use Liquitex Acrylic Varnishes?

  1. Ensure your acrylic painting is completely dry before application.
  2. Apply the varnish evenly using a brush, roller, or spray.
  3. Allow it to dry thoroughly for a flawless finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are these Acrylic Varnishes permanent?

Yes, they form a permanent, non-removable layer for long-lasting protection.

2. Do Liquitex Varnishes yellow over time?

No, they are formulated to resist yellowing and maintain clarity.

3. Can these varnishes be used on non-acrylic surfaces?

They are specifically designed for acrylic artworks but may be suitable for some mixed media applications.

4. What is the difference between the finishes?

  • Gloss: Reflective and vibrant.
  • Matte: Subtle and non-reflective.
  • Satin: A balanced finish with a slight sheen.
