Sennelier Oil Pastels and Sets

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Experience the pastel Picasso trusted - Sennelier Oil Pastels offer rich, creamy color that glides effortlessly across surfaces. Shop singles and sets now.

When it comes to oil pastels, Sennelier Oil Pastels are the gold standard. Developed in collaboration with none other than Pablo Picasso, these artist-quality pastels have a rich history and a reputation for unmatched quality. Known for their creamy texture, vibrant colors, and exceptional performance, these are a favorite among artists worldwide.

Let’s explore why these pastels are so special and how they can elevate your artwork.

What Are Sennelier Oil Pastels?

The oil pastels are made with the highest-quality pigments, combined with a pure synthetic binding medium and mineral wax. This unique formula gives them their smooth, buttery texture and intense color.

Here’s what makes them stand out:

  • Exceptional Color Vibrancy: High pigment content ensures bold, bright, and true-to-life colors.
  • Long-Lasting Quality: Excellent light stability, meaning your artwork will resist fading over time (except for metallic and fluorescent shades).
  • Creamy Texture: The pastels glide effortlessly, allowing for smooth blending and layering.
  • Professional-Grade Ingredients: Neutral pH wax and non-oxidizing binding medium ensure durability and stability.

Each pastel stick is paper-wrapped for easy handling and measures 2-5/8" × 3/8" (67 mm × 10 mm).

Why Choose These Oil Pastels and Sets?

Rich Heritage and Picasso’s Endorsement

The Sennelier Oil Pastels were created in collaboration with Pablo Picasso, who wanted a medium that combined the richness of oil paints with the ease of drawing. If they’re good enough for Picasso, they’re good enough for anyone!

Unmatched Color Quality

With their high pigment content, these pastels produce vibrant, bold colors with excellent coverage. Whether you’re creating detailed portraits or abstract landscapes, the colors will bring your work to life.

Versatile and Easy to Use

The creamy texture and smooth application make these pastels perfect for blending, layering, and experimenting. Use them on paper, canvas, or even wood!

Wide Variety of Sets

Sennelier offers a range of sets, from beginner-friendly options to extensive collections for professionals. Whether you want 6 colors or a whopping 120 shades, there’s a set for every artist.

Durability

Thanks to their non-oxidizing binding medium, these pastels retain their vibrancy and texture over time, making them a reliable choice for lasting artworks.

How to Use the Oil Pastels?

Using Sennelier Oil Pastels is simple, and they’re incredibly versatile. Here are some tips to get started:

Prepare Your Surface

These pastels work well on paper, canvas, wood, or even metal. Choose a surface that suits your project.

Blend and Layer

Use your fingers, blending tools, or brushes to mix colors smoothly. The creamy texture makes blending effortless.

Experiment with Techniques

Try layering for rich effects, or use a palette knife to create textured strokes.

Fix and Preserve

To protect your artwork, consider using a fixative designed for oil pastels.

FAQs

Q: Are the Oil Pastels good for beginners?

A: Yes! They’re easy to use, blend smoothly, and offer beginner-friendly sets to help you get started.

Q: Can I use Sennelier Oil Pastels on any surface?

A: They work best on paper, canvas, and wood. For unique projects, try experimenting with other surfaces like metal or glass.

Q: Do I need a fixative for my artwork?

A: While not required, a fixative can help protect your finished piece from smudging and dust.

Final Thoughts

Sennelier Oil Pastels are more than just a tool—they’re an experience. With their vibrant colors, creamy texture, and professional quality, they inspire creativity and allow you to bring your vision to life.
