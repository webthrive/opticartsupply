Blick Tempera Cakes – Easy, Washable Paint for Kids

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Mess-free painting made easy! Blick Tempera Cakes offer bold, washable color in a solid format - perfect for kids, classrooms, and creative play.

Whether you’re teaching a classroom of kids or enjoying creative time at home, Blick Tempera Cakes are an ideal choice for vibrant, mess-free painting. Designed for young artists, these solid tempera cakes are safe, easy to use, and long-lasting, making them perfect for colorful creations.

What Are Blick Tempera Cakes?

These are solid, concentrated tempera colors that activate instantly with a wet brush. Unlike liquid paints, these cakes are mess-free and easy to manage, making them ideal for classrooms and group projects.

Key Features:

  • Bright and Bold Colors: Vivid, opaque colors that stand out on paper.
  • Mess-Free: No spills or waste, perfect for young artists.
  • Safe and Non-Toxic: Designed with kids in mind, these paints are safe to use.
  • Reusable: Cakes re-harden when dry, making them long-lasting and economical.

Why Choose Blick Tempera Cakes?

1. Easy to Use

Just wet your brush, and the color is ready to go! There’s no need for extra preparation, making these cakes perfect for quick art sessions.

2. Mess-Free and Convenient

Say goodbye to spills and wasted paint. These cakes stay solid until activated with water, making them easy to distribute and clean up in group settings.

3. Vibrant, Mixable Colors

The bold primary and secondary colors can be mixed to create new hues, encouraging creativity and exploration.

4. Long-Lasting

Each cake provides enough color for hours of use. Once dry, they re-harden and can be stored without deteriorating.

5. Perfect for Kids

Safe and non-toxic, Blick Tempera Cakes are designed for young artists, making them great for classrooms or at-home art activities.

How to Use Blick Tempera Cakes

Here is a step-by-step guide on using the colors:

1. Wet Your Brush

Dip your brush in water and swirl it on the tempera cake to activate the color.

2. Start Painting

Apply the vibrant color directly to paper, poster board, or other surfaces.

3. Mix and Create

Combine colors on your palette or directly on your artwork to create custom hues.

4. Clean Up Easily

When finished, let the cakes dry before storing them. They’ll re-harden and be ready for the next use.
