What Are Pebeo Vitrea 160 Glass Paints?

The Vitrea 160 from Pebeo is a line of water-based glass paints designed for decorating glass surfaces. What sets these paints apart is their ability to create a durable, professional finish that mimics the look of stained glass when baked. Whether you’re a seasoned artist or a beginner looking to experiment, the Vitrea 160 offers versatility and ease of application.

Key Features of the Glass Paints

Heat-Set Durability

After painting, simply bake your glass item in a household oven at 160°C (325°F) for 40 minutes. This step makes the paint dishwasher-safe and resistant to wear and tear.

Wide Range of Colors

The line includes 35 vibrant colors, ranging from transparent and frosted finishes to shimmering metallics. These can be mixed to achieve endless possibilities.

Ease of Use

The paints are compatible with brushes, sponges, and even fine-tipped applicators for intricate details.

Non-Toxic Formula

Certified as safe, they’re suitable for use in schools or home projects with kids under supervision.

Why Choose Pebeo Vitrea 160 Glass Paints?

  1. Versatility: You can use these paints on a variety of glass items like vases, jars, glasses, and decorative plates. They’re also suitable for ceramics and porcelain surfaces.
  2. Customization Options: The paint’s compatibility with Pébéo’s range of outliners allows you to create unique, detailed designs. For example, outlining before filling in with colors can help you achieve a stained-glass effect.
  3. Professional Finish: The baked result is smooth, glossy, and durable, rivaling commercially produced glassware. It’s ideal for gifts, personalized home decor, or even selling your art.

How to Use Pebeo Vitrea 160 Glass Paints?

Prepare the Surface

Ensure your glass surface is clean and grease-free. Use rubbing alcohol or soapy water to remove any residues.

Plan Your Design

Sketch out your design beforehand or use stencils for precision.

Apply the Paint

Use a brush, sponge, or dropper for application. Experiment with blending colors for a unique effect.

Drying Time

Allow the paint to air dry for 24 hours before baking.

Bake

Place your item in a cold oven, set it to 160°C, and bake for 40 minutes. Let it cool inside the oven to avoid thermal shock.

Tips for Best Results

  • Shake the bottles well before use to ensure even pigmentation
  • Test colors on a spare piece of glass to understand their final appearance
  • Avoid painting areas that will come into contact with food or beverages
