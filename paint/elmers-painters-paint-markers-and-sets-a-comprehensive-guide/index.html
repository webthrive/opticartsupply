What Are Elmer's Painters Paint Markers?

The marker sets are high-quality, opaque markers filled with permanent acrylic paint. These markers are perfect for creating vibrant designs on a variety of surfaces, including:

  • Wood
  • Glass
  • Ceramic
  • Metal
  • Fabric
  • Paper

The paint is quick-drying, fade-resistant, and durable, making these markers ideal for both indoor and outdoor projects. Available in fine, medium, and broad tip sizes, they cater to detailed designs and larger coverage needs.

How to Use Elmer's Painters Paint Markers

Using Elmer's markers is straightforward. Follow these steps for the best results:

  1. Shake Well: Shake the marker vigorously to mix the paint.
  2. Prime the Tip: Press the tip down on scrap paper until the paint begins to flow.
  3. Start Crafting: Apply the marker to your chosen surface, layering as needed for richer color.
  4. Seal Your Work: For long-lasting results, consider sealing your project with a clear acrylic spray.

Pro Tip: Always test the marker on a small, inconspicuous area to ensure compatibility with the surface.

FAQs

1. Are Elmer's Painters Paint Markers safe for kids?

Yes, these markers are made with non-toxic materials and are safe for children to use under adult supervision.

2. Can these markers be used on fabric?

Yes, Elmer's Paint Markers work on fabric. For best results, let the paint dry completely and consider heat-setting it for added durability.

3. How do I store the markers?

Store horizontally with the caps tightly closed to prevent the paint from drying out.

4. Are the colors waterproof?

Yes, once dry, the paint is waterproof and fade-resistant, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor projects.
