Whether you’re just starting with pastels or have been creating for years, Pentel Oil Pastel Sets are a fantastic choice for artists of all levels. These pastels are known for their smooth application, vibrant colors, and versatility, making them a favorite for both beginners and professionals.

What Are Pentel Oil Pastels?

The Oil Pastels are colorful sticks designed for easy blending, smooth application, and long-lasting results. They work on various surfaces like paper, board, and canvas, giving you endless creative possibilities.

Key Features:

  • Vivid Colors: Bright, bold hues that make your artwork pop.
  • Smooth Application: Glides easily across surfaces for a seamless finish.
  • Fade-Resistant: Your colors stay vibrant over time.
  • Versatile Use: Perfect for blending, layering, and creating textures.
  • Durable Sticks: Each pastel measures 2-1/2", offering plenty of color for multiple projects.

Why Choose Pentel Oil Pastels?

Bright and Long-Lasting Colors

The high pigment content ensures every stroke is vivid and stays vibrant. These pastels are fade-resistant, so your artwork will look great for years to come.

Smooth and Blendable

The pastels glide effortlessly, making blending and layering easy. Create soft transitions or bold effects with minimal effort.

Variety of Sets

Versatile for All Levels

Beginners love the ease of use, while experienced artists appreciate the quality and range of colors.

Affordable and Reliable

Pentel offers professional-quality oil pastels at an affordable price, making them accessible to everyone.

How to Use Pentel Oil Pastels

Start with a Good Surface

These pastels work best on paper, canvas, or board. Experiment to find what suits your style.

Blend and Layer

Use your fingers or a blending tool to soften edges and mix colors seamlessly.

Add Texture

Experiment with Techniques
