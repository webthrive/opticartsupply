FAQs

1. Can I use these pencils without water?

Yes! Albrecht Dürer pencils perform exceptionally well as traditional colored pencils for detailed work.

2. Are the pencils refillable or sold individually?

Yes, individual pencils are available for purchase, allowing you to replace specific colors as needed.

3. What type of paper is best for these pencils?

Watercolor paper works best for activating pigments with water, but standard drawing paper is suitable for dry use.

4. How do I maintain the lightfastness of my artwork?

Use acid-free paper and keep your finished pieces out of direct sunlight to preserve their vibrancy.
