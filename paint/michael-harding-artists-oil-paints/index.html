Michael Harding Oil Paints – Handcrafted Color for Serious Artists

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Experience the color professionals trust - Michael Harding Oils are handmade for rich pigment, smooth flow, and lasting brilliance.

When it comes to creating stunning artwork, the quality of your materials makes all the difference. Michael Harding Oil Paint are a favorite among professional and aspiring artists alike, offering rich colors, excellent mixing power, and unmatched durability.

Let’s explore why these paints are a must-have for oil painters and how they can transform your creative experience.

What Makes Michael Harding Oil Paints Special?

These oil paints are crafted using traditional methods inspired by the Old Masters. Each tube is packed with pure, high-quality pigments, giving these paints their rich, vibrant color and exceptional blending ability.

Key Features:

  • High Pigment Load: Delivers vibrant, intense colors.
  • Silky Consistency: Perfect for smooth layers or thick impasto.
  • No Fillers or Driers: Ensures longevity without cracking or wrinkling.
  • Durable and Permanent: Made with neutral-acidity linseed oil for lasting results.

Whether you’re creating fine details or bold textures, these paints give you the control and quality you need.

Why Choose Michael Harding Oil Paints?

1. Unmatched Color Intensity

With high pigment concentrations, these paints produce bold, vibrant hues that remain true over time. They replicate the intensity of handcrafted oils from the 19th century, offering colors that pop off the canvas.

2. Silky and Versatile

The consistency of Michael Harding paints is silky, not oily. This makes them easy to work with, whether you’re applying thin glazes or thick, textured brushstrokes.

3. No Fillers or Driers

Unlike many other brands, Michael Harding uses no fillers or extenders. This means the colors are pure, durable, and won’t crack or wrinkle over time.

4. Crafted with Care

Each color of Michael Harding Oil Paint is ground in refined cold-pressed linseed oil, carefully chosen for its neutral acidity to ensure the longevity of your artwork.

5. Trusted by Artists Worldwide

Artists around the globe trust Michael Harding for the quality and reliability of their paints.

How to Use Michael Harding Oil Paints

Here is a simple guide on how to use the paints for the best result:

1. Prepare Your Surface

These paints work best on primed canvas or boards. Ensure your surface is clean and ready for oil painting.

2. Experiment with Techniques

Their silky texture makes them perfect for various techniques, from fine details to bold impasto strokes.

3. Mix Freely

Blend colors easily to create custom shades. The high pigment load ensures the colors stay vibrant, even when mixed.

4. Curing Your Artwork

Since no driers are added, thicker applications may take longer to cure. Place your painting in a warm room with good sunlight to optimize curing time.

Caring for Your Michael Harding Oil Paints

To get the most out of your paints:

  • Store Properly: Keep tubes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Cap Securely: Prevent drying by tightly closing the cap after use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are Michael Harding oil paints beginner-friendly?

A: Absolutely! Their silky texture and vibrant colors make them easy to work with for artists of all levels.

Q: Do these paints work on all surfaces?

A: They’re best suited for primed canvases or boards, but they can also be used on other oil-paint-friendly surfaces.

Q: Why do these paints take longer to dry?
