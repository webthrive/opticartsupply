What Are Procion MX Dyes?

Procion MX dyes are fiber-reactive dyes, meaning they form a chemical bond with natural fibers like cotton, linen, hemp, and rayon. This bonding ensures:

  • Vivid Colors – Bright, permanent results
  • Durability – Resists fading, even after multiple washes
  • Versatility – Great for tie-dye, batik, shibori, and hand painting

Jacquard’s Procion MX line is popular for its reliability and high-quality results, perfect for any skill level.

How to Use Procion MX Dyes?

Here’s a quick beginner’s guide:

Step 1: Prepare Your Workspace

Cover surfaces and wear gloves to prevent stains.

Step 2: Pre-Wash Fabric

Remove finishes that could resist dye absorption.

Step 3: Mix the Dye

Mix powder with water and add soda ash for bonding.

Step 4: Apply the Dye

Use your favorite technique—tie-dye, painting, immersion, etc.

Step 5: Fix the Color

Let the fabric set for 12–24 hours to ensure complete bonding.

Step 6: Rinse and Wash

Rinse until water runs clear, then wash with mild detergent.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Use cold water—Procion MX works best at room temp.
  • Always use soda ash to fix the dye.
  • Mix colors to create custom shades.
  • Store leftover dye solution in the fridge for 1–2 weeks.

FAQs

1. Are Procion MX dyes safe to use at home?

Yes, they are non-toxic and safe for home use with basic precautions like gloves and ventilation.

2. Can I use Procion MX dyes on synthetic fabrics?

No, they work best on natural fibers like cotton or rayon. For synthetics, try Jacquard’s iDye Poly.

3. How long do the dyes last after mixing?

Store dye solutions in the fridge—they’ll last 1–2 weeks.

4. Do I need to use soda ash?

Absolutely. Soda ash is essential for color fixation and long-lasting results.
