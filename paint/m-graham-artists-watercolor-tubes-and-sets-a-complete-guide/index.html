M. Graham Watercolor Tubes and Sets: A Complete Guide

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 2 min read

Paint with color that lasts - M. Graham Watercolors combine vivid pigment and honey-based moisture retention. Available in tubes and curated sets.

When it comes to choosing the right watercolor paints, quality can make all the difference. M Graham Watercolor Tubes and Sets have become a favorite among artists for their vibrant colors, smooth application, and commitment to eco-friendly practices. If you're considering investing in these watercolors, here's everything you need to know to make an informed decision.

What Makes M. Graham Watercolors Stand Out?

High-Quality Pigments

M Graham Watercolor Tubes are crafted with exceptional pigments that provide brilliant, long-lasting colors. Each hue is rich and luminous, offering excellent transparency and superior tinting strength.

Honey-Based Formula

Unlike many other brands, M. Graham uses a honey-based binder. This unique ingredient keeps the paint moist for longer periods, allowing artists to rewet dried paint easily. The honey also enhances color vibrancy and helps maintain a smooth, creamy texture.

Eco-Friendly and Non-Toxic

M Graham Watercolors are dedicated to creating environmentally friendly products. Their watercolors are non-toxic, making them a safe choice for artists who prioritize sustainability.

Available Options: Tubes and Sets

Individual Tubes

Perfect for artists who want to build a custom palette. M. Graham offers a wide range of colors in 15ml tubes, allowing you to select the shades that best suit your style.

Watercolor Sets

For beginners or artists looking for a curated selection, M. Graham provides thoughtfully assembled sets. Popular options include the Basic 5-Color Set, which features essential colors like Azo Yellow and Ultramarine Blue, and the expanded 10-Color Set for more versatility.

Who Are M. Graham Watercolors Best For?

Whether you’re a beginner or a professional artist, M. Graham’s watercolors are versatile enough to meet your needs:

Beginners

The honey-based formula ensures ease of use, making blending and layering straightforward.

Experienced Artists

Professionals appreciate the premium pigments and the brand's commitment to quality. These watercolors are ideal for detailed work, glazing, and large washes.

Tips for Using M Graham Watercolors

  • Use a Quality Brush: High-quality brushes will complement the smooth texture of M. Graham paints, giving you better control over your strokes.
  • Experiment with Layering: The transparent nature of the paints is perfect for glazing techniques.
  • Store Properly: Because of the honey-based formula, the paints can remain moist. Ensure tubes are tightly sealed to avoid leaks.
