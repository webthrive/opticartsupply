What Are Williamsburg Handmade Oil Paints?

The oil paints are premium-quality paints made using the finest pigments from around the globe. Each batch is carefully crafted to ensure the highest level of quality, with unique textures and a rich, vibrant finish.

Key Features:

  • 140+ Colors: A wide range of shades, each with its own texture and personality.
  • Small Batches: Made in small quantities to maintain precision and quality.
  • High-Quality Ingredients: Mixed with pH-balanced linseed oil for stability and durability.
  • Long-Lasting Colors: Most colors have excellent lightfastness, meaning they resist fading over time.

These paints are designed to give you a tactile, enjoyable painting experience while ensuring your artwork looks stunning.

How to Use Williamsburg Handmade Oil Paints

Prepare Your Surface

Use a primed canvas, wood panel, or prepared paper to get the best results.

Experiment with Texture

Explore the unique textures of each color. Use a palette knife for bold strokes or a soft brush for smoother finishes.

Layer and Glaze

Some colors are perfect for layering and glazing, letting you create depth and subtle effects.

Use a varnish to protect your painting and keep the colors vibrant over time.
