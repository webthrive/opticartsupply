Amaco Celadon Glazes - Glossy & Translucent Color Flow

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Bring out every carved detail with Amaco Celadon Glaze with glossy, translucent color that flows beautifully over texture. Shop vibrant colors now.

When it comes to ceramic art, the right glaze can elevate your creations to the next level. Amaco Celadon Glazes are a favorite for artists who want vibrant, glossy finishes that highlight textures and carved details. These glazes are easy to use, versatile, and available in a wide range of colors.

Let’s dive into what makes these glazes special and how they can enhance your ceramic projects.

What Are Amaco Celadon Glazes?

The glazes are lead-free, glossy, and transparent, designed to create vivid accents on textured or carved surfaces. These pre-mixed glazes are ready to use straight from the container, making them convenient for artists of all skill levels.

Key Features:

  • Glossy and Transparent: Highlights details beautifully.
  • 17 Vibrant Colors: Mix and match for endless creative possibilities.
  • Ready to Use: Pre-mixed for easy application on bisque-fired pieces.
  • Adjustable Transparency: Soften colors with C-11 Mixing Clear or add depth with layering.
  • Safe and Versatile: Lead-free and suitable for spray applications.

Why Choose Amaco Celadon Glazes?

1. Vibrant, Glossy Finishes

These glazes create stunning, transparent finishes that pool into carved or textured areas, adding depth and shine.

2. Easy to Use

Pre-mixed and ready to brush on, these glazes are perfect for both beginners and experienced ceramicists.

3. Mixable Colors

With 17 hues to choose from, you can create custom shades by mixing colors. Add depth with Obsidian or lighten tones with Snow.

4. Adjustable Transparency

Use C-11 Mixing Clear to soften colors or layer glazes for unique effects.

5. Lead-Free and Safe

All Amaco Celadon glazes are lead-free and safe for spray application, making them ideal for studio or classroom use.

Popular Colors in the Amaco Celadon Range

  • Obsidian: A deep black for shading or layering.
  • Snow: A pure white that can be used for tinting other colors.
  • Mixing Clear (C-11): Perfect for adjusting transparency and softening tones.
  • Additional Colors: Includes a vibrant palette of blues, greens, reds, and more, allowing for endless creativity.

How to Use the Glazes?

Using these glazes is straightforward and fun. Follow these simple steps:

1. Prepare Your Bisque-Fired Piece

Ensure your ceramic piece is bisque-fired and clean before applying the glaze.

2. Shake Well

Shake the glaze container thoroughly to ensure even distribution of color and consistency.

3. Apply the Glaze

Brush on 2–3 coats for the best results, allowing each coat to dry before applying the next. For spray application, thin with water and Amaco Gum Solution as needed.

4. Experiment with Layering

Layer Celadon glazes with other Amaco glazes for unique effects and textures.

5. Fire to High Temperature

Fire your piece in a kiln to cone 5 or 6 for the glossy, vibrant finish Celadon glazes are known for.

Tips for Stunning Results

  • Test First: Test your glaze combinations on a small piece to see how colors interact.
  • Mix and Match: Experiment with mixing colors to create custom shades.
  • Play with Transparency: Use C-11 Mixing Clear to achieve softer tones.

Final Thoughts

Amaco Celadon High-Fire Liquid Glazes are a fantastic choice for ceramic artists who want vibrant, glossy finishes with minimal effort. Their ease of use, mixable colors, and ability to highlight textures make them perfect for a wide range of projects. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned artist, these glazes offer endless creative possibilities.

FAQs

Q: Are these glazes food-safe?

A: Yes, Amaco Celadon glazes are lead-free and food-safe when fired to the recommended temperature.

Q: Can I mix these glazes with other brands?

A: While mixing with other Amaco glazes is recommended, you can experiment with other brands - just test on a small piece first.

Q: Do I need to thin these glazes before use?

A: These glazes are ready to use, but you can thin them with water and Amaco Gum Solution for spray applications.
