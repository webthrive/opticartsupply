How to Use Portfolio Oil Pastels?

Step 1: Choose Your Surface

These pastels work well on most papers, canvas, and textured boards.

Step 2: Apply Dry or Wet

Use Portfolio Water Soluble Oil Pastels dry for rich, bold strokes or blend with water for a watercolor effect.

Step 3: Experiment with Techniques

  • Dry Blending: Smudge colors together for smooth gradients.
  • Layering: Apply layers of color to build depth.
  • Watercolor Effect: Brush water over your drawing for soft, blended textures.

Step 4: Protect Your Art

Let wet areas dry completely. Use a fixative spray if you want to protect dry pastel work.
