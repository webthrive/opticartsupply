Setup & Care Tips

Assembly & Placement

Set cabinets on a level floor so drawers track smoothly. If stacking, align the corner guides and secure as instructed. Keep the top clear — it's useful as a layout surface, not a shelf.

Protecting Your Artwork

Interleave charcoal, pastel, and richly inked pieces with glassine. Use folders to keep small works inside rigid sleeves so nothing slides under larger sheets. Let varnishes and inks cure fully before filing to prevent sticking.

Cleaning & Maintenance

Wipe steel exteriors with a soft, barely damp cloth and dry immediately. Vacuum beneath and behind units to limit dust migration. If a drawer drags, clear the slide rather than forcing it.
