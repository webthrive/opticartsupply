Display Easel: A Guide for Artists, Businesses, and Event Planners

By Colin · 30 Jul 2025 · 3 min read

Capture attention and showcase with confidence - display easels are your go-to for a professional presentation. Find your perfect stand and upgrade your work.

Whether you’re organizing an art show, welcoming guests to a wedding, or simply showcasing a sign in your café, a display easel can help you do it with style and ease. These stands are designed not for creating artwork, but for presenting it. This easel adds a touch of professionalism to any setting, and the variety of styles available means there's one for nearly every need. This guide will walk you through what easels for display are, what to consider when buying one, and which brands to trust.

Understanding the role of a Display Easel

An easel for display is a stand used to present artwork, signs, posters, or boards. It isn’t meant for painting or drawing, but plays an important role in how art or messages are shared visually. From art galleries and schools to business conferences and weddings, these easels are incredibly versatile.

Key Purposes

  • Showcasing artwork: Instrumental in exhibitions, galleries, or student presentations.
  • Retail and hospitality: Used for displaying menus, sale signs, or promotions.
  • Events and weddings: Great for welcome signs, seating charts, or directional boards.

Features to Look for

When choosing a display easel stand, it’s helpful to know what features will best suit your needs. Here’s what to consider:

Stability and Size

  • Choose one that can hold your display material without tipping.
  • Make sure it fits the size of your poster, canvas, or board.

Material

  • Wooden easels for display offer a more traditional and elegant look.
  • Metal easels, often made of aluminum or steel, are lightweight and great for portability.

Adjustability and Storage

  • Look for height adjustability to match your display needs.
  • Some models fold flat, making them easy to store or transport.

Usability

  • Choose a tabletop display easel for smaller spaces or lighter items.
  • Consider models with added features like rubber feet, chalkboard attachments, or poster frames.

FAQs About Display Easels

Can I use this easel for painting?

Generally, no. Such easels for display are meant for holding artwork, not for creating it. They lack the stability and angle control needed for painting or drawing.

What’s the difference between a display easel and a studio easel?

Studio easels are designed for active use, painting and drawing, while display easels are built for showcasing finished work.

Are tabletop display easels sturdy?

Yes, most are. Just be sure to choose one made of quality material and with a design that supports your canvas or board securely.

Which is better: wood or metal?

It depends on your setting. Wood looks more classic and elegant. Metal is lighter and often more portable.

How do I choose the right size?

Check the dimensions of your sign, canvas, or poster. Your easel should support it without being too small or too large.
