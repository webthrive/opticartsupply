Blick Simplon Plein Air Frames: A Classic Choice for Your Artwork

By Colin · 21 May 2025 · 2 min read

Frame your plein air paintings with Blick Simplon Frames - lightweight, elegant, and ready to showcase your artwork with style.

When it comes to displaying artwork, the right frame makes all the difference. The Blick Simplon Plein Air Frame is a beautifully crafted, versatile option that enhances paintings, prints, and photographs. Whether you're an artist looking for a professional finish or someone wanting to showcase cherished pieces at home, this frame is a great choice.

Why Choose Blick Simplon Plein Air Frames?

These plein air frames are designed with a classic aesthetic that complements various art styles. Made from high-quality wood, they offer durability and elegance. The timeless design suits both modern and traditional artwork, making them a go-to option for galleries, studios, and homes alike.

Hand-Finished for a Unique Look

One of the standout features of these frames is the hand-finished gold and silver leaf options. Unlike mass-produced frames, these are crafted using the "Old World" technique, which creates thin separation lines between each leaf. This ensures that no two frames look exactly alike, giving your artwork a one-of-a-kind presentation.

Multiple Sizes and Profiles

The Blick Simplon Plein Air Frames come in two profile widths:

  • 5-inch profile: Features a 5/8" deep rabbet, suitable for slightly thicker artworks.
  • 3-1/4 inch profile: Has a 1/2" deep rabbet, perfect for standard-sized prints and paintings.

No matter your artwork's size, there’s a frame that fits your needs. The variety of finishes available allows you to match the frame seamlessly with your art and decor.

Understanding Frame Dimensions

When selecting a frame, it’s important to consider how it will fit your artwork. Here are a few key points to keep in mind:

  • Frame Size: Frames are measured by their opening, not the outer dimensions. If your artwork is 11" x 14", you should choose an 11" x 14" frame.
  • Overlap: The frame will cover about 1/4" around the edges of your artwork to hold it securely in place.
  • Rabbet Depth: This refers to how deep the frame is from front to back. It determines if your artwork will sit flush or slightly recessed within the frame.

By measuring your artwork properly before purchasing, you can ensure a perfect fit.

Open-Back Design for Flexibility

The Blick Simplon Plein Air Frames are open-back frames, meaning they do not come with glass, backing materials, or hanging hardware. This design is ideal for canvas paintings, panels, and stretched artwork. However, if you need additional support, you can easily add a backing board and hanging hardware separately.

Perfect for Artists and Collectors

These frames are not just for personal use. Many artists use them to frame their work for exhibitions, sales, and gallery displays. They provide a professional, high-end look that enhances the value of the artwork.

Collectors also appreciate these frames for their elegant yet simple design that complements various artistic styles. Whether you prefer landscapes, portraits, abstract pieces, or vintage prints, this frame enhances the visual appeal without overshadowing the art itself.
