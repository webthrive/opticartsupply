How to Use and Care for Your Silver Brush Silverwhite Soft Synthetic Brushes?

Before You Start Painting:

  • Lightly rinse new brushes with water to remove any factory residue.
  • Choose the right brush shape for your project—flats for broad strokes, rounds for detail.

While Painting:

  • Don’t overload the brush with paint; this helps maintain its shape.
  • Use gentle pressure to prevent damaging the bristles.

After Painting:

  • Rinse thoroughly with water (for water-based paints) or a brush cleaner (for oils).
  • Gently reshape the bristles with your fingers.
  • Lay flat to dry to prevent water from seeping into the ferrule.
