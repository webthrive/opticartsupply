Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes – Versatile Tools for Creative Expression

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 3 min read

Paint with confidence using Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes - durable, versatile, and affordable tools for acrylic, watercolor, and oil painting.

When it comes to art supplies, choosing the right brushes can make all the difference in the quality of your work. Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes are a popular choice among artists of all levels. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned professional, these brushes have a lot to offer. Here, we’ll explore what makes these brushes stand out and why they might be the perfect addition to your toolkit.

What Are Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes?

Simply Simmons Brushes is a line of affordable yet high-quality brushes made by Daler-Rowney, a trusted name in the art supply industry. These brushes are designed for artists who want reliable performance without breaking the bank. The synthetic bristles mimic the characteristics of natural hair, offering versatility and durability.

Key Features of Simply Simmons Brushes

Synthetic Bristles

  • Made to replicate natural hair, the bristles are smooth, flexible, and maintain their shape over time.
  • Ideal for various mediums like acrylics, watercolors, and oils.

Durability

  • The brushes are built to last, even with frequent use.
  • Synthetic fibers resist wear and tear better than natural hair.

Affordable Pricing

  • These brushes offer exceptional value for their quality.
  • Great for beginners or professionals looking to expand their collection without overspending.

Variety of Shapes and Sizes

  • Available in a wide range of shapes (round, flat, filbert, etc.) and sizes to suit different techniques and styles.

Comfortable Handles

  • Ergonomically designed handles ensure a comfortable grip, allowing for better control and precision.

Who Are These Brushes Best For?

Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes are ideal for:

  • Beginners: Affordable and versatile, these brushes are perfect for those just starting their artistic journey.
  • Intermediate and Advanced Artists: The quality and variety make them a solid choice for experienced artists looking for dependable brushes at a lower cost.
  • Mixed-Media Artists: Their synthetic bristles can handle different types of paints and techniques with ease.

Pros and Cons of Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes

Pros:

  • Affordable without sacrificing quality
  • Durable synthetic bristles suitable for multiple mediums
  • Available in a variety of shapes and sizes
  • Easy to clean and maintain

Cons:

  • May not perform as well as high-end professional brushes for specialized techniques
  • Synthetic bristles can feel slightly different from natural hair for those accustomed to traditional brushes

How to Care for Your Brushes?

To get the most out of your Simply Simmons Synthetic Brushes, follow these simple care tips:

  • Clean Immediately After Use: Use mild soap and lukewarm water to clean the bristles.
  • Shape the Bristles: After washing, reshape the bristles with your fingers to maintain their form.
  • Store Properly: Keep your brushes lying flat or upright in a container to prevent damage to the bristles.

Final Thoughts

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, these brushes are perfect for beginners due to their affordability and versatility across various mediums.

2. How do I clean the brushes?
