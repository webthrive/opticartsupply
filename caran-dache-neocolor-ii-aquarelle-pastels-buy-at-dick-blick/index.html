Caran d’Ache Neocolor II Aquarelle Pastels – Vibrant Water‑Soluble Wax Crayons

By Colin · 28 Apr 2025 · 3 min read

Caran d’Ache Neocolor II Aquarelle pastels are professional-grade, water-soluble wax crayons made in Switzerland. Their highly pigmented, dust‑free formulation offers rich coverage and versatility—usable dry for bold strokes or wet for watercolor effects on a range of surfaces

When it comes to high-quality art supplies, Caran d’Ache Neocolor II Aquarelle Artists' Pastels stand out as a favorite among professional artists, hobbyists, and art enthusiasts. These versatile water-soluble pastels combine the vibrancy of traditional crayons with the flexibility of watercolor, offering endless creative possibilities.

What Are Caran d'Ache Neocolor II Aquarelle Pastels?

Caran d’Ache Neocolor II is a line of water-soluble wax pastels created by the Swiss brand Caran d’Ache. Known for their vibrant colors, smooth application, and exceptional quality, these pastels are ideal for both dry and wet techniques. Unlike traditional pastels, Neocolor II combines the richness of wax crayons with the flexibility of watercolor paints, allowing you to create striking effects with minimal effort.

Key Features of Neocolor II Pastels

  • Vivid Color Range: With over 80 colors available, the Neocolor II collection offers an impressive spectrum of hues, from bright primaries to soft pastels and deep earth tones.
  • Water-Soluble: Transform your dry pastel strokes into watercolor-like washes by simply adding water with a brush.
  • High Pigment Concentration: The pastels deliver rich, opaque colors that work beautifully on various surfaces like paper, wood, and even canvas.
  • Durability: These pastels are designed to be long-lasting and resist breakage.
  • Non-Toxic: Safe for artists of all ages.

How to Use Caran d'Ache Neocolor II Pastels

Dry Application

Use them like traditional crayons for bold, vibrant marks.

Blending

Layer colors and blend them with your fingers for smooth transitions.

Watercolor Effects

Apply the pastel to your surface, then brush over it with water to create fluid, dreamy washes.

Mixed Media

Combine with other mediums like pencils, ink, or acrylics for unique textures and effects.

Who Are These Pastels For?

Neocolor II pastels cater to a wide range of users:

  • Professional Artists: Ideal for creating detailed illustrations, mixed media projects, or vibrant landscapes.
  • Hobbyists: Perfect for adult coloring books, card-making, or journaling.
  • Students and Beginners: Easy to use and forgiving for experimenting with techniques.
