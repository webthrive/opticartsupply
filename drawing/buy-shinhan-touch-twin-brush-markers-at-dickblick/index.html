What Are ShinHan Touch Twin Brush Markers?

The twin brush markers are alcohol-based markers designed for artists who value precision and vibrant colors. Manufactured by ShinHan Art, a trusted name in art supplies, these markers are dual-tipped - featuring a fine point for intricate details and a flexible brush tip for broader strokes.

With over 200 colors available, the Touch Twin Brush Markers provide endless possibilities for blending, shading, and layering.

Key Features of ShinHan Touch Twin Brush Markers

Dual Tips for Versatility

  • Fine Tip: Perfect for detailed line work and intricate designs.
  • Brush Tip: Ideal for smooth, broad strokes and seamless blending.

Alcohol-Based Ink

  • Dries quickly and provides streak-free application.
  • Allows for easy blending and layering without damaging paper.

Wide Color Range

Ergonomic Design

Tips for Using ShinHan Touch Twin Brush Markers

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are ShinHan Touch Twin Brush Markers refillable?

Yes, the markers are refillable, and replacement nibs are also available for purchase.

2. What type of paper works best with these markers?

Smooth, bleed-proof paper designed for alcohol-based markers works best to achieve optimal results.

3. How do ShinHan markers compare to Copic markers?

ShinHan markers offer similar quality to Copic markers at a more affordable price, though Copic has a larger color range and superior nibs.

4. Can beginners use these markers?

Absolutely! Their ease of use and affordability make them perfect for beginners exploring alcohol-based markers.
