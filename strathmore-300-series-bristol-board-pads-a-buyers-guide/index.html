What Are Strathmore 300 Series Bristol Board Pads?

The Strathmore Bristol Board Pads are high-quality, acid free drawing pads designed for artists who demand precision and durability. These pads feature smooth and vellum finishes, catering to different artistic needs:

  • Smooth Finish: Ideal for pen and ink, markers, and detailed illustrations.
  • Vellum Finish: Perfect for dry mediums like graphite, charcoal, and pastel, offering a textured surface for added depth.

Available in various sizes, these pads suit everything from small sketches to detailed large-format drawings.

Key Features of the Strathmore 300 Series Bristol

Premium Surface Quality

The Bristol board’s weight and texture ensure excellent pigment adhesion, preventing bleed-through and smudging.

Durability

With a 100 lb (270 gsm) paper weight, these pads withstand repeated erasing and layering without tearing.

Acid-Free for Longevity

Artists can trust that their work will remain vibrant over time, as the acid-free paper resists yellowing.

Eco-Friendly Manufacturing

Strathmore prioritizes sustainability, using responsibly sourced materials to create their products.

Tips for Using Bristol Pads

Match Your Medium to the Finish

Use the smooth finish for markers and fine lines. Opt for the vellum finish when working with textured tools like charcoal.

Work Cleanly

Keep a scrap sheet under your hand to avoid smudging your artwork, especially with graphite or pastel.

Cut and Frame with Ease

The sturdy paper can be cut to custom sizes and framed without warping.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What sizes are available for the 300 Series Bristol Board Pads?

A: They come in a variety of sizes, including 9x12, 11x14, and 14x17 inches, to suit different artistic needs.

Q: Can I use watercolor on the Strathmore 300 Series Bristol?

A: While the Bristol board is not designed specifically for watercolor, light washes can work on the smooth finish. For heavy watercolor techniques, consider Strathmore’s dedicated watercolor paper.

Q: Is the paper suitable for digital scanning?

A: Yes, the smooth finish provides a clean, crisp surface that scans beautifully, making it great for digital art workflows.

Q: Are the pads spiral-bound?

A: No, the Bristol Pads are glue-bound, making it easy to tear out individual sheets cleanly.

Q: Is the paper recyclable?

A: Yes, the acid-free and eco-friendly paper is recyclable, aligning with Strathmore’s commitment to sustainability.
