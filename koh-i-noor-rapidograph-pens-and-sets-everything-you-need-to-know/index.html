Koh-I-Noor Rapidograph Pens: Everything You Need to Know

By Colin · 27 Feb 2025 · 3 min read

Koh-I-Noor Rapidograph pens are well-known for their ability to produce precise, consistent lines. These technical pens are often used by architects, engineers, and illustrators for fine detail work.

If you’re an artist, designer, or technical professional, chances are you’ve heard of Koh-I-Noor pens. Renowned for their precision and quality, these pens are a favorite among professionals who demand accuracy and reliability. Whether you’re drafting intricate technical drawings or creating fine art, Rapidograph pens are designed to deliver. Let’s dive into what makes these pens stand out and why they might be the right choice for you.

What Are Koh-I-Noor Pens?

Koh-I-Noor Rapidograph pens are technical pens engineered for consistent line widths, making them ideal for detailed work. They feature:

  • Capillary ink cartridges that prevent clogging.
  • Fine stainless steel nibs for durability and precision.
  • A variety of nib sizes, ranging from ultra-fine (0.13 mm) to broader widths (1.20 mm), catering to different applications.

These pens are designed for use on various surfaces, including paper, drafting film, and tracing paper. Their versatility makes them popular across multiple disciplines.

Why Choose Koh-I-Noor Technical Pens​?

Here are some compelling reasons to consider these pens:

1. Unparalleled Precision

The consistent line widths are perfect for tasks where accuracy is non-negotiable. Architects, engineers, and illustrators often rely on these pens for clean, professional results.

2. Durable Build

Made with high-quality materials, including stainless steel nibs and sturdy plastic barrels, Koh-I-Noor pens are built to last. With proper care, they can serve you for years.

3. Refillable Ink System

Koh-I-Noor technical pens​ use refillable cartridges, making them an eco-friendly choice. You can replace the ink without needing to discard the entire pen, saving both money and waste.

How to Care for Your Koh-I-Noor Pens

To get the most out of your Rapidograph pens, proper maintenance is crucial:

Clean Regularly

Ink can dry and clog the nibs, so clean your pens after extended use. Koh-I-Noor provides cleaning solutions for this purpose.

Store Properly

Always store Koh-I-Noor pens horizontally to prevent ink from pooling in the nib.

Use Compatible Ink

Stick to Koh-I-Noor’s proprietary ink cartridges for optimal performance.
