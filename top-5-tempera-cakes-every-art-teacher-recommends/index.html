Top 5 Tempera Cakes Every Art Teacher Recommends

By Colin · 07 Oct 2025 · 4 min read

Bring color to the classroom with the top 5 tempera cakes teachers recommend—bright, durable, and student-friendly. Shop the best sets now.

Tempera cakes are a win for almost every teacher because they provide vibrant color, safe ingredients, and easy clean-up. These five teacher-approved brands deliver strength, brightness, and no-nonsense design for everyday art class.

What Are Tempera Cakes And Why Do Classrooms Need Them?

Tempera cakes are solid, water-soluble paints that need a little chunk of wet brush to activate and use bright, opaque color with ease - ideal for younger children as well as advanced art students.

Versatile and Easy to Use

Each cake is instantly re-wettable with water and snaps into a smooth, creamy version of itself. Teachers love that students can begin painting in seconds, with no need to pump, pour, or even mix.

Safe for All Ages

The tempera paint cakes are non-toxic and washable, so they will not harm skin, making them safe for even little users.

Long-Lasting and Economical

Tempera cakes do not dry out or spill like bottled paint; they can be used over and over again. One tray is good for many projects, so it's a great value. Makes one of the most budget-friendly and fun crafts.

Why Tempera Paint Cakes Are Perfect for Students and Teachers?

They streamline lessons and ensure teaching stays consistent, as your colors always remain the same, mix after mix. This convenience combines with practical creativity that every teacher can appreciate.

Clean, Efficient Classrooms

Tempera paint cakes are sold in compact trays, and there’s no waste or mess to clean up. Students are able to concentrate on painting rather than wiping up spills, which helps teachers make the most of their time.

Bright and Consistent Color

Every color remains vibrant after multiple reactivations. Educators like the way they mix cleanly without frustration for students learning to mix colors.

How to select the right Tempera Cakes Class Set?

Choosing the best set is contingent on classroom size, grade level, and storage space.

Match the Set to the Lesson

For younger students, smaller trays and pre-selected color groups work best. Upper-level classes might like the refillable racks---in warm and cool palettes for color theory.

Focus on Durability

Search for thick trays and refills that can withstand multiple washings. Sturdy tray for break resistance and longevity of product use.

Keep Safety and Cleanliness First

Be sure to always check that products are AP-certified, non-toxic, and washable. Trays air dry completely between uses in order to maintain a hygienic environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can kids use tempera cakes?

Yes. The majority of them are made from AP-certified, non-toxic materials that have been tested and meet the children’s product safety standards for use in schools with children of all ages.

How long do tempera cakes stay good for?

With proper maintenance, a set will last for months and may serve you well through an entire school year.

How are these cakes different from liquid tempera paint?

Tempera cakes are solid and are used with water, whereas liquid tempera simply needs some stirring.

Do you put them on canvas or wood?

Yes. For best results, use on primed surfaces that are oil-free, such as gessoed canvas or sanded wood panels..
