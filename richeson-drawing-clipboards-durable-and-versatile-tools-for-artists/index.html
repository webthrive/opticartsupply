Richeson Drawing Clipboards: Durable and Versatile Tools for Artists

By Colin · 27 Apr 2025 · 2 min read

Take your art anywhere with Richeson Drawing Clipboards - sturdy, lightweight boards with secure clips for sketching in the studio or outdoors.

Richeson Drawing Clipboards are designed to provide a reliable, sturdy surface for artists, students, and professionals. Crafted from strong hardwood with a smooth finish, these clipboards are the ideal companion for sketching, drafting, and drawing projects. Their practical design ensures that your artwork stays secure, allowing you to focus entirely on your creativity.

Why Choose Richeson Drawing Clipboards?

1. Premium Hardwood Construction

Each Richeson clipboard is made from durable, high-quality hardwood with rounded edges, ensuring a smooth and long-lasting surface that can withstand regular use.

2. Secure Pad Holders

Richeson Drawing Clipboards are equipped with:

  • Two heavy-duty clips to securely hold your drawing pad in place.
  • Oversized rubber bands to keep the edges of your paper steady while you work.

3. Smooth and Sturdy Drawing Surface

The clipboard’s polished finish provides an excellent surface for drawing or writing, making it perfect for various media, including pencils, pens, and charcoal.

4. Versatile Size Options

Choose from three sizes to suit your specific project requirements:

  • 11" x 17" for compact and portable use.
  • 18" x 18" for medium-sized projects.
  • 23" x 26" for larger, more detailed works.
