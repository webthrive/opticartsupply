What Is the Fredrix Pro Series Dixie Cotton Canvas?

Fredrix is a well-known brand that artists trust. The Pro Series Dixie Cotton Canvas is one of their best products. It’s made with 12 oz cotton duck canvas, known for its strength and quality.

  • Various Sizes: Available in a wide range of dimensions to suit any project.
