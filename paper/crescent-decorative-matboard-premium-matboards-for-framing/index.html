Crescent Matboard: Premium Decorative Boards for Framing

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 2 min read

Frame your art beautifully with Crescent Decorative Matboard - archival-quality mats in stylish colors and textures. Perfect for photos, prints, and artwork.

When it comes to preserving cherished memories or displaying artwork, the right matboard can make all the difference. Crescent Matboard is a premium choice for framing enthusiasts, offering a blend of aesthetics, quality, and durability. In this guide, we’ll explore what makes these matboards a top pick, how they elevate your framing projects, and why they’re worth considering for your next purchase.

What is a Crescent Matboard?

Crescent Decorative Matboards are specialized boards designed to complement and protect framed pieces such as photographs, artworks, and memorabilia. These matboards are crafted with precision, using high-quality materials to ensure longevity and visual appeal.

Whether you’re a professional framer, an artist, or a DIY enthusiast, Crescent offers a wide range of matboards that cater to various styles and preferences. From subtle textures to bold colors, the variety ensures there’s something for every project.

Key Features of Crescent Matboard

Premium Materials

The matboards are made from acid-free and lignin-free materials, ensuring they won’t yellow or deteriorate over time.

Vibrant Colors & Textures

Available in an extensive palette of colors and finishes, these boards add a professional touch to any frame.

Cutting Precision

Crescent boards are known for their consistent thickness and compatibility with precision cutting tools, making them ideal for intricate designs.

Eco-Friendly Options

Many of Crescent’s products are manufactured with environmentally responsible practices, appealing to eco-conscious buyers.

Benefits of Using a Crescent Decorative Matboard

Enhanced Presentation

Crescent matboards frame your artwork beautifully, drawing attention to the subject while adding a professional finish.

Long-Term Preservation

Acid-free properties help protect photographs and artwork from discoloration, ensuring your pieces look great for years to come.

Versatility

Whether framing a wedding photo or a fine art piece, Crescent’s variety of options makes it easy to find the perfect match.

DIY-Friendly

Easy-to-cut and handle, the matboards are a favorite for hobbyists and crafters looking to create custom frames.

How to Choose the Right Crescent Matboard

  • Consider the Artwork: For colorful or detailed art, opt for neutral-toned matboards to avoid overpowering the piece. For minimalist art, bold colors can add flair.
  • Match Your Frame: Coordinate your matboard with the frame material and color for a cohesive look.
  • Think About Lighting: Choose finishes and textures based on the room’s lighting to avoid glare or washed-out appearances.
