Cosmos Blotting Paper: Durable & Absorbent Sheets For Printmaking

By Colin · 30 Apr 2025 · 2 min read

Control moisture with Cosmos Blotting Paper - durable, absorbent sheets perfect for printmaking, bookbinding, and art preservation.

When it comes to keeping your skin fresh and shine-free throughout the day, Cosmos Blotting Paper has earned a reputation as a must-have in beauty bags worldwide. If you’re looking for an easy, effective way to control excess oil without disturbing your makeup, this product is a game-changer.

What is Cosmos Blotting Paper?

These blotting papers are a skincare product designed to absorb excess oil from your face, leaving your skin matte and refreshed. Unlike traditional tissue or towels, these papers are ultra-thin and specifically engineered to soak up sebum without smudging makeup or drying out your skin.

Made from natural fibers—like flaxseed or bamboo—these blotting papers are eco-friendly and gentle on your skin. They’re perfect for quick touch-ups, whether you’re at work, running errands, or heading to a special event.

The paper efficiently removes excess oil, keeping your skin balanced and shine-free.

Portable and Convenient

Lightweight packaging makes it easy to carry anywhere.

Eco-Friendly Design

How to Use These Blotting Papers?

  1. Take a single sheet from the package.
  2. Gently press it onto the oily areas of your face, focusing on the T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin).
  3. Avoid rubbing to prevent disturbing your makeup.
  4. Discard the used sheet responsibly.

Pro Tip: Use it mid-day or whenever you notice shine for a quick refresh.
