1. Sharp and Clear Images

This transparency film produces crisp, black-and-white or vibrant color images, depending on the type you use. Whether you're printing with a laser printer, an inkjet printer, or using a copier, your visuals will always look professional.

2. Versatile for Different Uses

This film isn’t just for projectors! Use it to print overlays, create art stencils, or design custom crafts. It’s perfect for schools, offices, and creative spaces.

3. Easy to Customize with Write-On Sheets

Need to make last-minute changes? The Write-On Transparency Sheets let you easily jot down notes or sketch ideas using transparency marking pens. Ideal for brainstorming sessions and interactive presentations.

4. High-Quality Coating for Consistent Results

Apollo’s special coating ensures that ink dries fast without smudging. This means your charts, graphs, and images come out clean and sharp every time.

5. Multiple Printing Options

Apollo Transparency Film offers transparency film options for both inkjet and laser printers, so you can choose what works best for your setup. Plus, copier-friendly options make bulk printing a breeze.

How to Use the Film?

Step 1: Choose the Right Film

  • Inkjet Film: For colorful visuals.
  • Laser/Copier Film: For black-and-white prints.
  • Write-On Film: For manual writing or drawing.

Step 2: Adjust Printer Settings

  • Select the appropriate paper type (transparency) in your printer settings.
  • Load the Apollo Transparency Film correctly—print on the coated side for the best results.

Step 3: Print and Let It Dry

  • Print your design and let it dry completely to avoid smudges.
  • For inkjet prints, drying is usually quick, but give it a minute to be safe.

Step 4: Present or Create

  • Place the film on an overhead projector or use it for your creative projects.
  • For Write-On sheets, use transparency markers to draw or write.

Tips for Best Results

  • Avoid touching the Printable Side: Oils from your fingers can affect the print quality.
  • Store Flat: Keep the sheets flat to avoid curling or bending.
  • Use Compatible Printers: Always match the film type with your printer for the best output.
  • Test Print First: For large projects, print a test sheet to check alignment and clarity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can Apollo Transparency Film be used in any printer?

A: No. Use the correct type for your printer - either inkjet or laser/copier film. The write-on version is not designed for printers.

Q: How do I remove ink smudges from the transparency film?

A: Gently wipe with a dry cloth. Avoid using water, as it may damage the print.

Q: Can I use regular pens on the write-on film?

A: No. You should use transparency markers to avoid smudging and ensure clear writing.
