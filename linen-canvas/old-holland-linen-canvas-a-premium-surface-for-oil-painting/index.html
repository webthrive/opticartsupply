Old Holland Linen Canvas: A Premium Surface for Oil Painting

Why Artists Choose This Canvas

Old Holland Claessens Linen Canvas starts with 100% Belgian linen woven from natural flax. Linen is known for its durability and smoother texture compared to cotton. It doesn't stretch out of shape easily and holds up well over time, which makes it perfect for oil painting.

The priming process here is different. This canvas is double-coated with an oil-based primer, applied by hand using a knife. Between coats, the primer is allowed to dry for three whole days. That slow, traditional method creates a surface that feels solid, smooth, and responsive under your brush.

The linen is stretched over 13/16-inch thick stretcher bars and back-stapled to keep the edges clean. The corners are neatly folded, which is great if you ever need to re-stretch the canvas later. Little details like this show the level of care that went into making this canvas.

FAQs

No, this canvas is oil-primed, specifically made for oil painting. Acrylics won’t bond properly with the surface.
