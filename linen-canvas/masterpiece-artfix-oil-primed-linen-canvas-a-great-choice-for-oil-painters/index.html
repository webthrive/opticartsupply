What Makes the Masterpiece Artfix Canvas Special?

Masterpiece is a trusted brand known for making top-quality stretched canvases. Their Artfix Oil Primed Linen Canvas is loved by many artists because of its high-quality materials and expert craftsmanship.

1. Hand-Primed for a Smooth Surface

This canvas is hand-primed, which makes a big difference in how paint goes on. It is treated with:

  • Two coats of size to protect the linen.
  • One coat of oil-based primer to create a perfect painting surface.

This prevents paint from soaking through, so colors stay bright and vibrant. The smooth surface also makes painting easier and more enjoyable.

2. Made with High-Quality Belgian Linen

The canvas is made from 100% Belgian linen, which is known for being strong and smooth. It has a 13 oz primed weight, giving it a firm but flexible feel.

3. Strong Wooden Frame

A strong frame keeps a canvas tight and stable. This canvas features:

  • A tongue-and-groove bracing system, which holds the frame together without glue or staples.
  • Cross-bracing for larger sizes (16" x 20" and up) to prevent warping.
  • Durable wood that keeps the canvas in great shape over time.

This means your canvas will stay strong and won’t sag, even after years of use.

4. Clean, Staple-Free Edges

Unlike many stretched canvases, this one has no staples on the sides, giving it a neat and professional look. The folded corners and extra fabric allow you to re-stretch the canvas if needed.

Why Choose Linen Over Cotton?

When picking a canvas, you usually have two options: cotton or linen. Cotton is common and affordable, but linen is stronger, smoother, and lasts longer. Here’s why many artists prefer linen:

  • More Durable – Linen is made from flax fibers, which are stronger than cotton. This makes the canvas less likely to stretch or sag over time.
  • Great for Oil Paints – The smooth texture helps paint glide on easily and makes blending simple.
  • Long-Lasting – Linen is archival-quality, which means your paintings will stay in great shape for years.

If you want a high-quality canvas that improves your painting experience, linen is the best choice.

FAQs

Can I use acrylic paint on the Artfix Oil Primed Linen Canvas?

No, this canvas is specially primed for oil paint only. Acrylics won’t adhere properly and may cause peeling over time.

What makes hand-primed canvas better than machine-primed?

Hand-primed canvas offers a smoother, more controlled surface. It prevents paint from soaking through and enhances color vibrancy and brush control.

Does this canvas come with cross-bracing for larger sizes?

Yes. For sizes 16" x 20" and up, it includes cross-bracing to prevent warping and maintain long-term tension.
