What Makes Old Holland Claessens Oil Primed Belgian Linen Canvas Special?

Not all linen canvases are the same. The Old Holland Claessens Oil Primed Belgian Linen Canvas goes through a careful preparation process to create the best painting surface. Here’s why it stands out:

  • 100% Belgian Linen – Made from natural flax, this canvas is strong and has a beautiful texture.
  • Oil-Based Primer for a Perfect Finish – Instead of using an acrylic primer, this canvas is coated with two layers of oil-based gesso. This makes it perfect for oil paints, allowing them to glide smoothly across the surface.
  • Hand-Applied Primer – Each layer of primer is carefully spread by hand using a knife, creating an even and high-quality surface.
  • Slow Drying Process for Better Quality – After each layer of primer, the canvas dries for three days to prevent cracking and ensure a strong, long-lasting surface.
  • Strong Wooden Frame – The linen is stretched over thick 13/16" wooden bars, which keep the canvas stable and prevent warping.
  • Neat and Professional Look – The canvas is stapled at the back, keeping the edges clean so it looks great in any setting.
  • Easy to Restretch – The corners are carefully folded, making it simple to adjust or restretch if needed.

If you want a high-quality, professional canvas that will last for years, this one is a fantastic choice.

Why Choose Belgian Linen?

Belgian linen is one of the best materials for oil painting because it is stronger and more durable than cotton. Here’s why artists love it:

  • Long Lasting – Linen is much tougher than cotton, so your artwork will stay in good shape for years.
  • Works Great with Oil Paints – Oil paint sticks well to linen, helping you create smooth, rich, and vibrant colors.
  • Doesn’t Warp or Stretch Easily – This canvas won’t absorb too much moisture, which keeps it from changing shape over time.
  • Ideal for Detailed Work – The fine, even texture of linen makes it perfect for both smooth brushstrokes and bold textures.

If you want your paintings to look professional and last a long time, Belgian linen is a great choice.
