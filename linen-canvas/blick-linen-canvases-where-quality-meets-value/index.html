FAQs

Can I use oil paint on the Blick Studio Linen Canvas?

While it’s primed with acrylic gesso (which is best suited for acrylics), you can use oil paint if you add an oil primer or an isolation layer before painting.

What’s the difference between acrylic and oil priming?

Acrylic priming creates a flexible surface suitable for both acrylics and oils (with prep), while oil priming gives a more absorbent surface ideal for oil paints and offers better adhesion and archival quality for oils.

Do I need to frame these canvases?

Not necessarily. Both canvases have clean, staple-free edges, so you can display them without a frame for a modern, gallery-style look. But they’re also easy to frame if you prefer a finished presentation.
