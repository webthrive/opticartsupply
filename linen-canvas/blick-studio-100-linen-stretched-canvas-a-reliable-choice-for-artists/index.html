Why Choose Linen Over Cotton?

One of the biggest decisions artists face when choosing a canvas is whether to go with cotton or linen. While cotton is more common and budget-friendly, linen offers some key advantages that make it worth considering:

Durability

Linen fibers are stronger and more resistant to stretching and sagging over time. This means your artwork will stay in top shape for years.

Texture

Many artists prefer the natural texture of linen, which provides a slightly rougher, more absorbent surface compared to cotton.

Archival Quality

If you want your artwork to last for generations, linen is the better option. It holds up well under different environmental conditions and retains paint beautifully.

With the Blick Studio 100% Linen Stretched Canvas, you get all these benefits at a price that won’t break the bank.
