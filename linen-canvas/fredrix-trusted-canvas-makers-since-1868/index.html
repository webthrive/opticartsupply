Fredrix: Trusted Canvas Makers Since 1868

By Colin · 21 May 2025 · 2 min read

For over 150 years, Fredrix has been a trusted name in canvas. Known for their commitment to quality and craftsmanship, Fredrix continues to deliver professional-grade surfaces that artists can count on. Their Pro Series Belgian Linen Canvas is a standout, bringing together time-tested materials and modern performance in a way that feels effortless for the artist.

If you're searching for a reliable, archival canvas with a smooth, responsive surface, Fredrix is a brand that delivers.

Why Artists Choose Fredrix

Fredrix has built its reputation on consistency and care. Each canvas in the Pro Series line is made with high-quality Belgian linen, a material artists have trusted for centuries. It’s stronger and longer lasting than cotton and holds paint beautifully.

Fredrix pairs this top-grade linen with kiln-dried stretcher bars from sustainably harvested wood, and every canvas is primed in the USA to meet the needs of today’s oil and acrylic painters.

Whether you work tight and detailed or loose and expressive, Fredrix provides the kind of canvas that keeps up with your brush—and your vision.

Spotlight: Pro Series Belgian Linen Canvas

This canvas is where premium materials and expert craftsmanship meet.

  • Made with 5.5 oz Belgian linen, known for its durability and fine texture
  • Triple-primed with acid-free acrylic titanium gesso for a ready-to-paint surface
  • Primed weight of 11 oz, ideal for both oils and acrylics
  • Stretched by hand on 7/8" profile kiln-dried stretcher bars
  • Extra bracing added on larger sizes for strength and support
  • Back-stapled with clean edges for frameless or gallery-wrapped display

It’s suitable for oils, acrylics, water-soluble oils, alkyds, paint markers, and even spray paints. This kind of flexibility makes it a go-to for studio artists, teachers, and professionals alike.

Crafted for Long-Term Use

Every Fredrix Pro Series canvas is made with archival-quality materials. The acrylic gesso is acid-free, and the linen is built to hold up over time without warping, yellowing, or sagging.

Fredrix also puts care into the support system. The stretcher bars are sourced from regional, sustainable forests and are kiln-dried to stay true through years of use. That kind of attention to detail matters, especially when you're creating work meant to last.

FAQs

Is Fredrix Pro Series linen suitable for beginners?

Yes. While it’s built to meet the standards of professionals, beginners who want a better-quality surface will appreciate how easy it is to work on.

Can I use water-based paints like acrylics?

Absolutely. The acrylic gesso primer works great with acrylics, as well as oils and other water-soluble paints.

Are the canvases ready to paint out of the box?

Yes, they come pre-stretched and pre-primed. You can start painting right away.
