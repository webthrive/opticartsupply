White Foam Board: Lightweight, Versatile, and Easy to Use

By Colin · 27 May 2025 · 3 min read

Create professional displays with White Foam Board - lightweight, smooth, and easy to cut for mounting, framing, and presentations.

The White Foam Board is an essential tool for artists, designers, and educators. The boards are made from a polystyrene foam core and laminated with white coated-paper stock on both sides. These board combines durability with a virtually weightless design. If you're framing artwork, creating tabletop displays, or crafting three-dimensional projects, this foam board is your versatile companion.

Why Choose White Foam Board?

1. Lightweight and Durable

The white craft board's polystyrene foam core offers exceptional strength while remaining lightweight, which makes it easy to handle for projects of all sizes.

2. Multi-Purpose Design

This foam board is ideal for:

  • Framing and Backing: A sturdy back-up for large framed works.
  • Poster Mounting: Ensures a smooth, professional presentation.
  • Tabletop Displays and Signs: Perfect for school projects, business presentations, or events.
  • 3D Crafting: Easily scored and folded for dimensional projects.

3. Easy to Cut and Customize

The White Foam Board cuts cleanly with a utility or mat knife and allows precise customization for your creative needs.

Product Specifications

  • Material: Polystyrene foam core with white coated-paper stock.
  • Thickness: Standard 3/16" (5 mm).
  • Sizes Available: Includes large formats like 40" × 60" (102 cm × 153 cm) for oversized projects.
  • Shipping Note: Oversized packages may require truck shipment and do not qualify for free shipping.
