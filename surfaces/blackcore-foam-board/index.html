Blackcore Foam Board – Strong & Stylish Boards for Mounting and Presentation

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Make your artwork pop with Blackcore Foam Board - rigid, lightweight, and stylish for photos, signs, and creative projects.

When it comes to creating displays or signs that stand out, Blackcore Foam Board is a fantastic option. With its bold black core and double-sided black finish, it adds an elegant and dramatic touch to any project. Whether you’re designing for presentations, exhibitions, or DIY crafts, the board offers durability, style, and flexibility.

What Is Blackcore Foam Board?

The foam board is like a regular board, but with a twist - it has a black foam core and is laminated on both sides with black, coated paper stock. This design not only looks sleek but also enhances your displays by providing a professional, polished background.

Available Sizes:

  • 3/16" (5 mm) thickness: Lightweight and easy to handle, perfect for most projects.
  • 1/2" (13 mm) thickness: Offers added durability and support for larger or heavier displays.

This board is ideal for creating signage, mounting artwork, and crafting standout presentations.

Why Choose Blackcore Foam Board?

Elegant Appearance

The all-black design gives a dramatic and professional look, making your work pop. It’s perfect for displays that need a touch of sophistication.

Versatile Uses

From signage and exhibitions to photo mounting and crafts, the board adapts to a wide range of projects.

Lightweight Yet Sturdy

Despite its solid appearance, this foam board is lightweight, making it easy to carry and install. It’s also sturdy enough to hold up for long-term displays.

Easy to Work With

The smooth surface and soft foam core make it simple to cut, shape, and customize. You can use utility knives or crafting tools for precise designs.

Professional-Grade Material

The coated paper stock not only enhances the look but also provides durability and resistance to wear and tear.

How to Choose the Right Thickness?

Picking the right thickness depends on your project needs:

3/16" Thickness (5 mm)

  • Best for lightweight projects like indoor signs, small displays, and crafts.
  • Easy to handle and cut, perfect for quick and simple designs.

1/2" Thickness (13 mm)

  • Ideal for larger or more durable displays, like trade show signage or long-term presentations.
  • Provides extra support for heavier mounting projects.

How to Use the Foam Board?

Using the Blackcore Foam Board is easy, even for beginners. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Plan Your Design

Sketch out your ideas and measurements before cutting.

Cut to Size

Use a sharp utility knife or foam board cutter for clean edges. Make sure to cut on a stable surface.

Attach Your Materials

Use adhesive, double-sided tape, or mounting spray to secure photos, posters, or prints.

Customize Your Display

Add lettering, graphics, or decorations to make your project unique.

Set Up Your Display

Mount or place your foam board in your desired location. Its lightweight design makes it easy to handle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use Blackcore Foam Board outdoors?

A: It is best for indoor use. While durable, prolonged exposure to moisture or sunlight may affect its finish.

Q: What tools are best for cutting this foam board?

A: A sharp utility knife or foam board cutter works best for clean and precise edges.

Q: Is this foam board reusable?

A: Yes! With proper care, the foam board can be reused for multiple projects. Just avoid bending or damaging the edges.

Final Thoughts

Blackcore Foam Board is an excellent choice for anyone looking to create professional and striking displays. Its sleek black design, lightweight build, and versatility make it a favorite among artists, designers, and business professionals.

Whether you’re crafting a simple sign or a detailed presentation, this foam board provides the durability and style you need. With options for different thicknesses, it’s easy to find the right fit for your project.
