General's Charcoal Pencils

Creating art is all about having the right tools. If you’re an artist or just starting with sketching, General's Charcoal Pencils and Sets are an excellent addition to your toolkit. Known for their quality and versatility, these charcoal pencils provide the depth and precision you need to bring your ideas to life.

What Are General's Charcoal Pencils and Sets?

General’s is a trusted brand in the art world, offering top-notch materials for professional and budding artists alike. Their charcoal pencils stand out for their highly refined willow charcoal and organic blacks, making them smooth, rich, and easy to use.

Each pencil is crafted with a cedar casing for durability and comfort. The pencils come pre-sharpened, so you can start drawing immediately.

  • Classroom Assortment, Set of 72: Features 24 pencils each of 2B, 4B, and 6B, perfect for group settings like art classes.

Convenience

Pre-sharpened pencils save time, and the included accessories—erasers and sharpeners—make these sets all-in-one solutions for artists on the go.

Durable Design

Encased in cedar wood, the pencils are sturdy and comfortable to hold. This makes long sketching sessions more enjoyable.

How to Use General's Charcoal Pencils

  1. Prepare Your Materials: Start with a good-quality drawing paper that complements charcoal.
  2. Select Your Pencil Grade:
    • Use HB for light outlines.
    • Switch to 2B, 4B, or 6B for shading and darker tones.
    • Use the Charcoal White pencil to add highlights.
  3. Blend and Erase: Blend with your fingers or a blending tool for smooth transitions. Use the kneaded eraser to clean up smudges or create highlights.
  4. Sharpen as Needed: Keep your pencils sharp with the included All-Art Sharpener for precise lines.
