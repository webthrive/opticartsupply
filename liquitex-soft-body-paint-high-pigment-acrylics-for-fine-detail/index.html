Liquitex Soft Body Paint: High-Pigment Acrylics for Fine Detail

By Colin · 18 Oct 2025 · 3 min read

Soft, smooth, and full of pigment—Liquitex Soft Body Acrylics offer the perfect balance for artists who love precision and flow.

Golden Fluid Acrylic Paint possesses excellent leveling qualities, so it spreads smoothly and evenly on paper, canvas, or board. It has the same pigment load as heavy body acrylics, but in a much lower viscosity. Professional results should be expected, and you can create both fine details and large compositions.

What Makes Golden Fluid Acrylic Paint Set a Studio Favorite?

The Golden brand fluid acrylic paint stands out for its pure pigments and smooth consistency, which enhance control and clarity in every brush stroke. It performs beautifully for brushing, staining, glazing, or airbrushing without sacrificing vibrancy or durability.

Smooth Flow and Balanced Consistency

Its well-formulated viscosity allows the paint to move evenly across surfaces, reducing streaks and keeping lines clean. Whether used for detailed work or broad layers, the result stays consistent.

Pure Pigment Strength

Each color in the paint line contains concentrated, lightfast pigments with no added fillers. This ensures a clean, brilliant tone that lasts over time.

Reliable Surface Compatibility

It can be applied to canvas, wood, paper, and fabric with a brush or spatula, and also makes a flexible film when dry. It is also crack and fade-resistant, even under conditions of frequent handling or extreme environmental exposure.

Golden Fluid Acrylics are specially formulated to be more fluid and transparent than Golden Heavy Body Acrylics while retaining the same high pigment concentration. Even though it is recommended that you layer, glaze, or fine finish on a flat surface.

  • Retains brilliance in thin washes or transparent layers
  • Dries flexible, water-resistant, and permanent
  • Compatible with brushes, pens, and airbrushes

The fluid build allows for supreme accuracy, delivering even colour coverage with the lightest laydown and laser precision application, which means nothing can get in the way of creating your perfect look.
