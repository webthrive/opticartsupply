Kraft Paper Tape

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Whether you’re packing boxes, working on art projects, or sealing cartons, Kraft Paper Tape is a durable and eco-friendly choice. Known for its strength and versatility, this traditional brown tape is a must-have for artists, DIY enthusiasts, and anyone looking for a reliable sealing option.

Let’s explore what makes Kraft Paper Tape a standout product, how to use it, and why it’s worth adding to your toolkit.

What Is Kraft Paper Tape?

Kraft Paper Tape is a 60 lb weight, brown packaging tape that requires moistening to activate its adhesive. It’s designed to provide a strong, long-lasting seal and can be used for various tasks, from packaging to stretching watercolor paper for art projects.

Key Features:

  • Strong Adhesion: Provides a secure seal for boxes, cartons, and other surfaces.
  • Multi-Purpose: Great for packaging, art projects, and more.
  • Eco-Friendly: Made from paper, it’s biodegradable and recyclable.
  • Long Roll: A single roll measures 600 ft (183 m), ensuring you have plenty to work with.
  • Compact Core: Features a 1½" (38 mm) core for easy handling.

Why Choose Kraft Paper Tape?

1. Reliable and Durable

This tape is tough enough to handle heavy-duty tasks like sealing boxes for shipping. Its moist-activated adhesive creates a bond that lasts.

2. Eco-Friendly Alternative

Unlike plastic tape, Kraft Paper Tape is biodegradable and recyclable, making it an environmentally friendly choice for packaging and projects.

3. Versatile Use

From securing parcels to prepping watercolor paper for painting, this tape is perfect for a variety of creative and practical applications.

4. Easy to Use

Although it requires moistening, this tape is simple to apply. A quick swipe with a damp sponge or brush activates the adhesive.

5. Cost-Effective

With 600 feet of tape on a single roll, you get excellent value for money.

How to Use Kraft Paper Tape

1. Activate the Adhesive

Use a damp sponge, brush, or cloth to moisten the adhesive side of the tape.

2. Apply to Surface

Place the moistened tape on your desired surface, pressing firmly to ensure a strong bond.

3. Let It Dry

Allow the adhesive to set for a secure and long-lasting hold.

4. Store Properly

Keep the roll in a dry place to ensure the adhesive remains effective.

Creative Uses for Kraft Paper Tape

While it’s often used for packaging, Kraft Paper Tape is also a favorite among artists and crafters:

  • Stretching Watercolor Paper: Secure wet watercolor paper to a drawing board to prevent buckling as it dries.
  • Framing and Matting: Use it for mounting artwork or securing backing in frames.
  • Craft Projects: Ideal for creating clean, eco-friendly seals on handmade packaging or gift wrapping.
  • Labeling: Write directly on the tape for a rustic, personalized look.

Where to Use Kraft Paper Tape

This tape is ideal for:

  • Shipping and Packaging: Seal boxes and cartons securely for shipping or storage.
  • Art Studios: Stretch watercolor paper or secure materials for art projects.
  • Home and Office: Use it for organizing, labeling, or craft projects.

FAQs

Q: Can Kraft Paper Tape be recycled?

A: Yes, it’s biodegradable and recyclable, making it an eco-friendly choice.

Q: How do I activate the adhesive?

A: Simply moisten the adhesive side with a damp sponge or brush before applying it.

Q: Can it hold heavy items?

A: Absolutely. Its strong adhesion is designed for heavy-duty tasks like sealing shipping boxes.
