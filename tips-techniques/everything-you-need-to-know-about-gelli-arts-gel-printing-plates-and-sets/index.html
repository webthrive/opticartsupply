Everything You Need to Know About Gelli Arts Gel Printing Plates and Sets

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 3 min read

Gel printing has taken the art and craft world by storm, and Gelli Arts is a name that stands out for its innovation and quality. Whether you’re a seasoned artist or someone exploring creative outlets, Gelli Arts gel printing plates and sets are versatile tools that make printmaking an enjoyable and accessible process. This guide dives deep into what makes these products unique, how to use them, and why they’re a must-have for your art supplies.

What Are Gelli Arts Gel Printing Plates?

Gelli Arts gel printing plates are reusable, flexible, and non-toxic printing surfaces made specifically for monoprinting. Unlike traditional printmaking methods that require bulky equipment, these plates are portable and easy to use.

Key Features:

  • Reusable and Long-Lasting: Designed for frequent use with proper care.
  • Non-Toxic Materials: Safe for adults and kids alike.
  • Compatible with Various Media: Use with acrylic paints, inks, and even natural elements like leaves or textured fabrics.
  • No Press Required: Create intricate prints without heavy machinery.

Why Choose Gelli Arts Gel Printing Sets?

Gelli Arts doesn’t just offer individual plates—their sets include everything you need to start printing right out of the box. These sets are curated to inspire creativity and provide endless possibilities.

Popular Gelli Arts Gel Printing Sets:

  • The Starter Kit – Perfect for beginners, this set includes a gel plate, a brayer, and a tube of paint.
  • The Deluxe Kit – Comes with additional tools like stencils and texture combs for more advanced projects.
  • The Classroom Pack – A bulk option ideal for group workshops and classrooms.

Each set is thoughtfully designed, ensuring you have the right tools to experiment and create unique prints.

How to Use Gelli Arts Gel Printing Plates

Gel printing is intuitive and doesn’t require advanced skills. Here’s a quick step-by-step guide:

  1. Prepare Your Workspace: Lay down a protective surface and gather your plate, brayer, paint, and stencils (optional).
  2. Apply Paint: Spread a small amount of acrylic paint evenly over the plate using a brayer.
  3. Add Texture: Use stencils, natural elements, or household objects to create patterns on the plate.
  4. Print: Place a sheet of paper over the plate, press lightly, and peel it off to reveal your print.
  5. Clean and Store: Wipe the plate with a damp cloth or baby wipe and store it flat in its original packaging.

Pro Tip: Experiment with layers and different types of paper to achieve stunning effects.

FAQs

1. Are Gelli Arts gel printing plates safe for kids?

Yes, Gelli Arts plates are non-toxic and safe for kids to use under adult supervision.

2. How do I clean my gel printing plate?

Simply wipe the plate with a damp cloth or baby wipe after each use. Avoid using harsh chemicals.

3. Can I use other paints besides acrylics on the plates?

While acrylic paints are most commonly used, you can experiment with other water-based paints and inks.

4. Where can I find Gelli Arts products?
