Gamblin Gamvar Varnish

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 2 min read

When you finish a painting, you want it to look its best and last for years. That’s where Gamblin Gamvar Varnish comes in. Designed with artists in mind, this varnish protects your work while making colors pop. It’s easy to use, odorless, and gives your painting a professional, polished finish.

Let’s explore why it’s a favorite among artists and how it can make a difference in your work.

What Is Gamblin Gamvar Varnish?

Gamblin Gamvar Varnish is a top-quality varnish made in collaboration with the National Gallery of Art. It’s specially designed to enhance and protect oil and acrylic paintings.

Key Features:

  • Color Enhancement: Makes colors look deeper, richer, and more vibrant.
  • Protective Coating: Shields your painting from dirt, dust, and UV light.
  • Easy to Apply: Comes premixed and ready to use with a brush.
  • Removable: Can be safely removed with Gamblin Gamsol, making conservation easy.

Gamvar Varnish is available in three finishes: Gloss, Matte, and Satin, so you can choose the look that best suits your artwork.

Why Choose Gamblin Gamvar Varnish?

Brings Colors to Life

Gamvar Varnish saturates colors, adding depth and brilliance. It’s perfect for making your painting look finished and professional.

Protects Your Artwork

This varnish creates a protective layer that keeps dust and dirt away while also reducing the risk of UV damage.

Safe and Odorless

Unlike some varnishes, Gamvar is virtually odorless, making it more pleasant to use in your workspace.

Removable for Conservation

Over time, varnishes can yellow or collect dirt. Gamvar can be easily removed with Gamblin Gamsol, making it ideal for long-term care.

Multiple Finishes

  • Gloss: Offers the highest level of color saturation and a shiny surface.
  • Matte: Reduces glare while maintaining deep color values.
  • Satin: A balance between gloss and matte for a soft sheen.

How to Use Gamblin Gamvar Varnish

Using Gamvar Varnish is simple, but it’s important to follow these steps to get the best results:

Check Your Painting

Make sure the thickest areas of your painting are dry and firm to the touch. For oil paintings, this might take a few months.

Prepare Your Workspace

Work in a clean, dust-free area with good ventilation.

Apply the Varnish

Use a soft, flat brush to apply a thin, even layer. Avoid spraying, as it’s not recommended.
For best results, apply in one direction and avoid over-brushing.

Let It Dry

Allow the varnish to dry completely before handling or displaying your artwork.

FAQs

Q: What is the difference between Gamvar Gloss, Matte, and Satin?

A: Gloss gives a shiny finish and deep color saturation. Matte reduces glare with a flat finish. Satin is a middle ground with a soft sheen.

Q: How long should I wait before applying Gamvar?

A: Wait until the thickest parts of your painting are fully dry and firm to the touch. For oil paintings, this may take a few months.

Q: Can I remove Gamvar Varnish later?

A: Yes, Gamvar can be safely removed with Gamblin Gamsol, making it perfect for long-term care and conservation.

Final Thoughts

Gamblin Gamvar Varnish is an excellent choice for any artist looking to protect and enhance their work. Whether you prefer a glossy shine, a soft satin look, or a matte finish, Gamvar has you covered.

With its easy application, odorless formula, and protective qualities, this varnish is a must-have for preserving your art.

If you want your artwork to look its best and stand the test of time, try Gamblin Gamvar Varnish. Your paintings deserve it!
