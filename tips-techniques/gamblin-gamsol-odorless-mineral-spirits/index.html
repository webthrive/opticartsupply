Gamblin Gamsol Odorless Mineral Spirits

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 2 min read

Gamblin Gamsol Odorless Mineral Spirits is a trusted solvent for oil painters. These mineral spirits are less toxic than traditional turpentine and are ideal for cleaning brushes without affecting bristle quality.

If you’re an artist working with oil paints, a high-quality solvent is essential. Gamblin Gamsol Odorless Mineral Spirits is a trusted choice among professionals and beginners alike. Known for its effectiveness and safety, the spirit is a versatile tool for thinning mediums, cleaning brushes, and maintaining a healthier studio environment.

Let’s explore why this odorless mineral spirits are a favorite for oil painters and how they can make your painting process smoother and safer.

What Is Gamblin Gamsol?

Gamsol mineral spirits are high-quality, odorless mineral spirits designed for oil painting. Unlike traditional solvents like turpentine, it is less toxic because it has had the harmful aromatic solvents removed.

Key Features:

  • Evaporates Completely: Leaves no residue in your paint layers.
  • Odorless: Makes your studio more comfortable to work in.
  • Safer Formula: Free of harmful aromatic solvents, making it less toxic than other mineral spirits.
  • Multi-Purpose: Use it to thin mediums, clean brushes, and maintain your tools.

Gamblin Gamsol combines effectiveness with safety, making it a go-to solvent for artists who care about their work and their health.

Why Choose These Mineral Spirits?

Gentle on Your Health

Gamsol odorless mineral spirits is designed with safety in mind. It’s free of aromatic solvents, which makes it much less harmful compared to traditional mineral spirits or turpentine.

Odorless for a Better Workspace

Painting should be enjoyable, not hard and challenging. The spirit is odorless, so you don't get any harsh smells in your studio.

Evaporates Completely

Unlike some solvents, it evaporates fully, leaving no residue behind. This ensures your paint layers are clean and long-lasting.

Versatile Use

From thinning oil paints and mediums to cleaning brushes and tools, Gamblin gamsol is a multi-purpose solution for every artist.

Trusted by Professionals

Gamblin is a respected name in the art world, known for creating products that prioritize artists’ safety and the quality of their work.

How to Use Gamblin Gamsol Odorless Mineral Spirits

Using the product is easy, and the versatility makes it a valuable tool for any oil painter:

1. Thinning Mediums and Paints

  • Mix it with oil paints to adjust their consistency and flow.
  • Combine it with Gamblin mediums like Galkyd for smoother applications.

2. Cleaning Brushes

  • Rinse your brushes in Gamsol mineral spirits to remove oil paint effectively.
  • For deeper cleaning, follow up with soap and water to keep your brushes in top shape.

3. General Studio Cleanup

  • Use it to wipe down palettes or clean up spills.
  • It’s effective for cleaning tools and surfaces without leaving a sticky residue.

Ready to elevate your painting experience? Try it and enjoy a safer, more enjoyable way to create.

Why Safety Matters with Solvents

Traditional solvents like turpentine can release harmful fumes and leave residue, which isn’t ideal for your health or your artwork. Gamsol odorless mineral spirits minimizes certain health risks:

Reduced Toxicity

Harmful aromatic solvents are removed, making it safer to use in enclosed spaces.

No Lingering Smell

The odorless formula improves your working environment.

While the product is safer than other options, it’s still a solvent. Always use it in a well-ventilated area and follow proper safety guidelines.
