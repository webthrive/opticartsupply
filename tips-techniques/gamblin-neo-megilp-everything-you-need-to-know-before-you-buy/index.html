Gamblin Neo-Megilp: Everything You Need to Know Before You Buy

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 3 min read

When choosing the right medium for your oil painting, the options can be overwhelming. One name that consistently stands out among artists is Gamblin Neo-Megilp. This versatile medium has earned a stellar reputation for its unique qualities and ability to transform the painting experience. Let’s dive into what makes Gamblin Neo-Megilp a standout choice for oil painters.

What Is Gamblin Neo-Megilp?

Gamblin Neo-Megilp is a contemporary oil painting medium designed to replicate the properties of traditional Megilp—a historic medium made from linseed oil and mastic resin. Unlike its predecessor, Neo-Megilp is free from the yellowing and cracking issues associated with traditional formulas. This makes it a safer and more durable choice for modern artists.

Key Benefits of Using Gamblin Neo-Megilp

Silky Consistency

Neo-Megilp creates a soft, gel-like consistency that’s easy to manipulate on the canvas. This texture is ideal for creating smooth, flowing brushstrokes and subtle blending.

Enhances Transparency and Depth

If you’re aiming for luminous glazes or want to build layers of depth in your painting, Neo-Megilp delivers. It enhances the transparency of your oil paints while maintaining their richness.

Dries to a Satin Finish

The medium dries with a beautiful satin finish, giving your artwork a polished and professional look without excessive glossiness.

Improved Durability

Formulated without natural resins, Neo-Megilp resists yellowing over time. This ensures that your colors remain vibrant and true for years to come.

Safe and Modern Formula

Unlike traditional mediums, Gamblin Neo-Megilp is made with safer, modern materials that are less prone to deterioration. This aligns with Gamblin’s commitment to environmental responsibility.

Who Should Use Gamblin Neo-Megilp?

Gamblin Neo-Megilp is perfect for:

  • Artists looking for a reliable alternative to traditional mediums
  • Painters who enjoy working with soft, fluid textures
  • Those who want to create layers of glaze or achieve an even, satin finish

Whether you’re a beginner exploring oil painting or a seasoned professional, Neo-Megilp can elevate your artwork.

How to Use Gamblin Neo-Megilp

Using Neo-Megilp is straightforward:

  1. Mix it with your oil paints directly on your palette.
  2. Adjust the ratio to achieve your desired texture and transparency. Start with a small amount and gradually add more.
  3. Apply the mixture to your canvas using a brush or palette knife.

The medium works well with a wide range of oil paints, including Gamblin’s Artist’s Oil Colors. For more tips, check out Gamblin’s official guide on using their products.

Gamblin is a trusted name in the art supplies industry. Their dedication to producing high-quality, eco-friendly materials has made them a favorite among artists. The company prioritizes:

  • Safety: Their products are designed to reduce exposure to harmful chemicals.
  • Education: Gamblin provides a wealth of resources to help artists make informed choices. For more insights, visit their resource library.

FAQs

1. Can Neo-Megilp be used with all types of oil paints?

Yes, Neo-Megilp works well with all traditional oil paints, enhancing their consistency and transparency.

2. How long does it take for Neo-Megilp to dry?

Drying time varies based on the thickness of application, but it typically dries faster than traditional oil mediums.

3. Is Neo-Megilp safe to use indoors?

Yes, it’s formulated with safer ingredients, making it suitable for studio use without strong odors.

4. Does Neo-Megilp yellow over time?

No, Neo-Megilp is designed to resist yellowing, ensuring your artwork stays vibrant for years.
