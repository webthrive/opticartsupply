Artist Tape

By Colin · 22 May 2025 · 3 min read

Whether you’re working on an intricate art project, labeling supplies, or organizing your studio, Artist Tape is an essential tool. With its clean removal and versatile features, this tape is perfect for artists, students, and anyone who loves staying organized without the mess.
Let’s explore why Artist Tape is a go-to choice for creatives and how it can make your projects easier and more polished.

What Is Artist Tape?

Artist Tape is a special type of adhesive tape designed for delicate surfaces. It has a moderate to low tack, meaning it sticks well but can be removed easily without damaging your paper, canvas, or other surfaces.

Key Features:

  • Easy to Remove: Peels off cleanly without tearing or leaving residue.
  • Writable Surface: The smooth white surface is perfect for labeling, notes, or instructions.
  • Durable Roll: Each roll is 60 yards long and fits on a 3-inch core, giving you plenty to work with.

Why Choose Artist Tape?

Gentle on Surfaces

Unlike regular tape, Artist Tape won’t damage your work. It’s safe to use on paper, watercolor boards, and even delicate sketches.

No Sticky Residue

Tired of cleaning sticky marks after removing tape? With Artist Tape, there’s no residue left behind.

Versatile and Easy to Use

From masking off areas for painting to labeling tools and supplies, this tape does it all. It’s easy to apply and even easier to remove.

Writable Surface

Need to make quick notes or label items? The smooth white surface is perfect for writing with pens, pencils, or markers.

Great Value

Each roll is 60 yards long, ensuring you have enough for multiple projects without constantly running out.

Creative Ways to Use the Tape:

For Art Projects

  • Masking Areas: Use the tape to block off sections of your canvas or paper for clean, crisp lines.
  • Framing Edges: Keep borders neat and free from paint or pencil smudges.
  • Temporary Fixes: Secure paper or lightweight objects without worrying about damage.

For Organization

  • Labeling Supplies: Mark jars, brushes, or toolboxes for easy identification.
  • Color Coding: Combine with colored markers to organize your materials by type or purpose.

For Everyday Use

  • Planning: Use it in bullet journals or planners for removable notes or section dividers.
  • Household Projects: Perfect for labeling storage boxes, cords, or shelves.

How to Use the Tape

Using Artist Tape is simple:

Cut or Tear

Measure the length you need and cut with scissors or tear gently by hand.

Apply

Press the tape onto your chosen surface. Its moderate tack ensures it stays in place without slipping.

Remove

Peel it off slowly when you’re done. The tape comes off cleanly, leaving no sticky residue behind.

A: No, it’s designed to be gentle and remove cleanly without tearing or leaving residue.

Q: Can I write on Artist Tape?

A: Yes! The smooth white surface is perfect for writing with pens, pencils, or markers.

Q: How long is each roll?

Final Thoughts

Artist Tape is a simple yet incredibly useful tool for anyone who values precision, organization, and creativity. Whether you’re masking edges for a painting or labeling your art supplies, this tape makes your work easier and cleaner.
Its gentle adhesive, writable surface, and long-lasting rolls make it a smart choice for artists, crafters, and even everyday use at home or in the office.
