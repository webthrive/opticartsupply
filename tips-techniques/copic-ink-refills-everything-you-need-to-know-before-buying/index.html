Copic Ink Refills: Everything You Need to Know Before Buying

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 3 min read

If you’re into art, illustration, or crafting, you’ve probably heard of Copic markers — the gold standard in the creative world. Loved by professionals and hobbyists alike, these markers stand out for their vibrant colors, blendability, and durability. However, like any tool, they eventually run out of ink. Enter Copic ink refills — a sustainable and cost-effective solution to keep your creativity flowing without having to replace your markers. Here’s everything you need to know about them.

What Are Copic Ink Refills?

Copic ink refills, officially called Copic Various Ink, are designed to replenish the ink in your Copic markers. These refills come in 358 colors, perfectly matching the entire range of Copic markers. Each bottle contains 12 ml of ink, which can refill a marker multiple times:

  • Copic Classic: ~7 refills per bottle
  • Copic Sketch: ~8–10 refills per bottle
  • Copic Ciao: ~15 refills per bottle

This refill system not only saves money but also reduces waste, aligning with the brand's commitment to sustainability.

Why Choose Copic Ink Refills?

Cost-Effective

A single bottle of Various Ink is much cheaper than replacing multiple markers.

Eco-Friendly

Reduce plastic waste by refilling instead of buying new markers.

Customizable

Blend inks to create custom colors.

Convenient

Easy to use with minimal mess.

How to Refill Your Copic Markers

Refilling your markers might seem intimidating at first, but it’s incredibly simple. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Identify the Color: Ensure the refill color matches your marker. Copic’s numbering system makes this easy.
  2. Remove the Nib: Use tweezers or your fingers to gently pull out the nib.
  3. Add Ink: Use the dropper tip of the Various Ink bottle to drip ink directly into the marker barrel. Avoid overfilling.
  4. Reinsert the Nib: Push the nib back in and let it sit for a few minutes to absorb the ink.
  5. Test the Marker: Check for smooth ink flow and consistent coloring.

For more detailed instructions, visit Copic’s official guide.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Refills

  • Store Properly: Keep your refills in a cool, dry place to extend their shelf life.
  • Label Your Bottles: Avoid mix-ups by labeling each refill with the marker’s color code.
  • Use an Ink Scale: For precision, weigh your marker before and after refilling to ensure the right amount of ink.
  • Experiment with Blends: Mix inks to create unique shades for your projects.

Final Thoughts: Are Copic Refills Worth It?

Absolutely. Whether you’re a professional illustrator or a hobbyist, Copic ink refills are a game-changer. They’re not just a way to extend the life of your markers but also an investment in your art journey. With their vibrant colors, ease of use, and eco-friendly benefits, Copic refills are a must-have for any creative toolbox.

Still on the fence? Consider starting with a few essential colors to see the difference for yourself. Happy coloring!

FAQs

1. How many times can I refill a Copic marker with one bottle?

It depends on the marker type: Copic Classic (~7 refills), Sketch (~8–10 refills), and Ciao (~15 refills).

2. Are Copic ink refills compatible with all Copic markers?

Yes, Copic Various Ink refills work with Copic Classic, Sketch, and Ciao markers.

3. Can I mix Copic inks to create custom colors?

Absolutely! Copic inks are designed to be blendable, making it easy to create unique shades.

4. Where can I buy genuine Copic ink refills?

You can purchase them from authorized sellers like Dick Blick or directly from Copic’s official website.
