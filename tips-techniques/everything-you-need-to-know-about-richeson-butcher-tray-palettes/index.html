Everything You Need to Know About Richeson Butcher Tray Palettes

By Colin · 24 May 2025 · 3 min read

When it comes to finding the perfect palette for your painting needs, the Richeson Butcher Tray Palette is a popular choice for both beginners and professional artists. This durable and versatile tool has earned its reputation as a must-have for those who value convenience and efficiency in their creative process. Let’s dive into what makes this palette stand out and why it could be the right choice for you.

What is a Richeson Butcher Tray Palette?

The Richeson Butcher Tray Palette is a sturdy metal tray coated with a white, glossy enamel finish. It’s rectangular with a slight curvature at the edges to keep paints and water in place. This design is simple yet functional, making it ideal for mixing watercolors, acrylics, gouache, and other water-based media.

The smooth enamel surface offers an excellent mixing area, allowing colors to blend effortlessly without soaking into the palette. This makes cleanup quick and easy—a feature every artist can appreciate!

Key Features of the Richeson Butcher Tray Palette

Durability

Made from heavy-gauge steel, this palette is built to last.

Versatile Mixing Surface

Its non-porous enamel coating ensures your paints don’t bead up, allowing for smooth, even mixing.

Spacious Design

Available in multiple sizes, you’ll have plenty of room for mixing large quantities of paint or creating intricate color gradients.

Easy to Clean

Simply rinse with water or wipe down with a damp cloth after use.

Benefits of Using the Richeson Butcher Tray Palette

  • Enhanced Color Mixing: The glossy white surface reflects light, making it easier to see true paint colors.
  • Multi-Medium Compatibility: Works well with watercolors, acrylics, and gouache.
  • Portability: Lightweight and easy to transport.
  • Affordable: Offers excellent value for money.

Who Should Use the Richeson Butcher Tray Palette?

  • Beginners: Its simplicity and ease of use make it ideal for those new to painting.
  • Professionals: The ample mixing space and durable design cater to the needs of seasoned artists.
  • Crafters and Hobbyists: Great for DIY projects and experimenting with various mediums.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Butcher Tray Palette

Prevent Staining

Clean it immediately after each use to keep the enamel surface looking pristine.

Pair with Proper Brushes

Use high-quality brushes for a seamless painting experience on the smooth surface.

Experiment with Sizes

Choose a size that best fits your workspace and painting style.

FAQs

1. What sizes are available for the Richeson Butcher Tray Palette?

The Richeson Butcher Tray Palette comes in various sizes, typically ranging from 7” x 11” to 11” x 15”.

2. Can I use oil paints with this palette?

While it’s designed for water-based media, some artists do use it for oil paints—though it requires more thorough cleaning.

3. How do I clean the enamel surface effectively?

Use warm water and a soft sponge. For stubborn stains, a mild detergent works well.

4. Is the Richeson Butcher Tray Palette suitable for travel?

Yes! Its lightweight and durable design makes it ideal for plein air painting and travel.
